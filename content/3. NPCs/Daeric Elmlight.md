### **Daeric Elmlight**

Firbolg Merchant

![[Pasted image 20260427223850.png|454]]

Daeric Elmlight is a soft-spoken, broad-shouldered Firbolg in his middle years, known throughout [[Longsaddle]] for his calm demeanor, honest trade, and the distinct golden mead produced from his family’s own apiary. Descended from a druidic Firbolg clan that once lived in the High Forest, Daeric chose a quieter, more practical life than that of his ancestors.

He runs a farm and apiary where he peddles grains, wines, and mead.  Specifically Elmlight Gold. He has a wife, Marta, and three kids: Tirna, Orlem, and Bree.
