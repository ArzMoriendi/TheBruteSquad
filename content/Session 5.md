### Scene Setter
Our party decides to take a rest and several things happen at once.  A haze descends across the group and Kallíspallí disappears. All party members have no memory of her and their memories of her part of the party are now attributed to other members. Loreli, B'Imbo, Leyawiin, Zwargie, and Shadow Scale are contacted by deities in their dream and have new powers imparted on them.  The party levels up.

Continuing our adventure with our party, our group of intrepid explorers breach the door that they unlocked. On the other side, they encounter 8 sleeping shapes (guards and bandits).  B'Imbo attempts to stealthily travel through the sleeping group, but unfortunately wakes up a sleeping guard.  This initiates a melee. Zwargie, Shadow Scale, and Artiran each deal a healthy amount of damage, each killing an enemy or two, while Leyawiin, B'Imbo, and Loreli send spells and magical support.  Leyawiin lands the killing blow, if igniting a guard on fire.  

Loot: a pretty rock, a scrap of cloth, fish bones, a scimitar and a short sword (Loreli takes all of this).

Continuing on, our adventures make their way through a passage that opens out on to another room with two bandits throwing food into the mouth of a large ogre sitting on a blue crate. Loreli offers hard candy to the group. Zwargie stealths into the room, behind the ogre.  Leyawiin casts sleep on one of the bandits, immediately knocking him out, but the other one resists. The rest of the group proceeds to attack, quickly killing the other guard and slowly killing the ogre.  They attempt to tie up the guard, but he wakes and resists, and the group kill him quickly. 

Loot: 1 potion of healing. 2 x Platinum, 22 GP, 11 SP

Total Loot:  1 potion of healing. 2 x Platinum, 22 GP, 11 SP. a pretty rock, a scrap of cloth, fish bones, a scimitar and a short sword

They move on, finding a shaft that leads outside to a small grove.  The group takes a long rest.
