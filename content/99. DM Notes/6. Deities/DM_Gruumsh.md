Here’s a detailed profile of **Gruumsh**, the chief deity of the orcs in Faerûn, based exclusively on official Dungeons & Dragons 5e sources (like the *Player’s Handbook*, *Mordenkainen’s Tome of Foes*, *Volo’s Guide to Monsters*) and verified lore from the *Forgotten Realms Wiki*:

---

## **Gruumsh** — *The One-Eyed God, Lord of Battle, Destroyer of Elves*

### 🛐 **Portfolio, Domains & Responsibilities**

* **Portfolio**: Orcs, war, conquest, survival, strength, tribal unity, and the relentless drive to dominate. Gruumsh embodies the orcish spirit of raw power, battle frenzy, and unyielding hatred toward elves.
* **5e Divine Domains**: *War*, *Tempest* (from *Player’s Handbook* and *Mordenkainen’s Tome of Foes*), reflecting his nature as a war god and bringer of storms and destruction.
* **Responsibilities**: He leads orcs in their endless campaigns of conquest and survival, emphasizing strength, endurance, and tribal loyalty. Gruumsh inspires orcs to overcome adversity, break their enemies, and assert dominion over their lands.

---

### 🎨 **Symbols, Appearance & Avatar**

* **Symbol**: A single, jagged eye (often stylized as a bloodshot or flaming eye), sometimes accompanied by a broken spear or claw marks. The most iconic image is a simple, stylized eye shape representing his lost eye.
* **Appearance**: Usually depicted as a towering, muscular orc with a single glaring eye in the center of his forehead. Sometimes shown wielding a massive spear or great axe, often wearing crude armor and war paint.
* **Avatar**: In the mortal realm, Gruumsh may appear as a fierce, towering orc warrior, one-eyed and radiating raw martial power and rage.

---

### 📜 **History & Lore**

* **Origins**: Gruumsh is one of the oldest deities in Faerûn’s orc pantheon, traditionally considered the chief god of the orcs. According to myth, during the war between gods and giants, he lost his eye in battle, a symbol of sacrifice and hatred—especially toward elves, who are his eternal foes.
* **Mythic Rivalry**: His most famous adversary is the elven god Corellon Larethian, from whom Gruumsh lost his eye. This loss sparked a bitter, endless conflict between orcs and elves in Faerûnian myth.
* **Role in Orc Culture**: Gruumsh is the unifying divine figure in orc society, driving them toward strength, unity, and conquest. Orc tribes invoke his name before battle and offer blood sacrifices to gain his favor.

---

### ⚔️ **Adversaries & Allies**

* **Adversaries**:

  * *Corellon Larethian*, leader of the elven pantheon and Gruumsh’s arch-enemy.
  * *Sehanine Moonbow* and other elven deities allied with Corellon.
  * *Sune* and other deities of beauty and peace often oppose Gruumsh’s destructive goals.
* **Allies**:

  * *Bahgtru*, *Luthic*, and *Shargaas* — fellow orc deities who support war, fertility, and stealth.
  * *Lolth* (in some interpretations), especially in alliances against elves and other common foes.
  * Orc war chiefs, marauders, and warlords draw strength from Gruumsh’s favor.

---

### 🧭 **Worship, Clergy & Practices**

* **Worshipers**: Orcs primarily, but also half-orcs, and some goblinoids who respect or fear Gruumsh’s warlike nature. His followers include shamans, warpriests, and berserkers.
* **Clergy & Orders**: Priests of Gruumsh are fierce warriors who combine spiritual rites with brutal martial prowess. Their rituals often involve bloodletting, war dances, and communal feasts.
* **Temples & Shrines**: Often crude, outdoor shrines in or near orc encampments or war camps—marked by bones, skulls, and trophies from enemies. Sacred groves and battlefields are considered holy places.
* **Rituals**:

  * **The Eye’s Blood Rite**: A rite performed before battle involving the spilling of blood to renew Gruumsh’s favor.
  * **Hunting of the Elven Stag**: A ritual reenacting the divine enmity against elves, symbolizing the orc struggle for survival and dominance.
