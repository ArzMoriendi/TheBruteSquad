## **Lathander** — *The Morninglord, Lord of Birth and Renewal*

### 📜 Titles & Alignment

* **Titles**: The Morninglord; Commander of Creativity; Inspiration’s Dawn; The Rose‑and‑Gold God; Bringer of the Dawn; Patron of Spring & Eternal Youth; Mentor of Self‑Perfection ([Forgotten Realms][1])
* **Alignment**: Neutral Good ([Forgotten Realms][1])

---

### 🛐 Portfolio & Domains

* **Portfolio**: Dawn, birth, youth, renewal, spring, vitality, creativity, self‑perfection, athletics ([World Anvil][2])
* **5e Domains**: *Life*, *Light* ([Forgotten Realms][1])

---

### 🎨 Symbol, Colors & Avatar

* **Symbol**: A road leading into a sunrise—or a stylized sunrise made of rose‑red and yellow gems ([Forgotten Realms][1])
* **Colors**: Rose, gold, violet—dawn hues used in iconography and temples ([World Anvil][2])
* **Divine Avatar**: Often depicted as a youthful, radiant figure draped in robes of dawn‑colors, with golden hair and tan skin—appearing lyrical and inspiring ([Reddit][3])

---

### 📖 Lore, History & Responsibilities

* While **not a solar god**, Lathander is the deity of the dawning light and all beginnings—not literally the sun but its promise at first light ([World Anvil][2]).
* Devotees pray to Lathander before embarking on ventures, signing contracts, or beginning new chapters—socially and personally ([World Anvil][2]).
* He champions civilization and creative growth—but despises undead, viewing them as perversions of life and renewal ([World Anvil][2]).
* Central to his lore is the **Dawn Cataclysm**—a near-divine upheaval in which he attempted to reshape the pantheon in his image. The resulting chaos claimed several deities (including Murdane, Helm’s beloved) and sowed cosmic instability. Helm holds a lasting grudge over this event ([World Anvil][4]).

---

### 🤝 Allies & Adversaries

* **Allies**:

  * *Chauntea* (romantic partner, shared affinity for growth)
  * *Kelemvor, Tyr, Torm, Ilmater, Sune, Gond, Mielikki, Oghma, Tymora, Selûne* (co‑partners in life, justice, artistry, nature) ([World Anvil][4])
* **Foes**:

  * *Cyric, Talos, Shar, Bane, Malar, Velsharoon*—forces of destruction, darkness, tyranny, or antithesis of rebirth ([realmshelps.net][5])

---

### 🧮 Practices & Worship

* **Worshipers**: Merchants, artists, athletes, merchants, Sun Soul monks, the young, communities unveiling new ventures or projects ([Forgotten Realms][1]).
* **Clerical Alignments**: Lawful & Neutral & Chaotic Good (LG, NG, CG) ([nwnarelith.com][6]).
* **Favored Weapon**: *Dawnspeaker*, a mace (light or heavy) symbolizing his radiance and martial support of life   ([nwnarelith.com][6]).
* **Holy Days & Rituals**:

  * *Song of Dawn* festivals at Vernal & Autumnal Equinoxes and Midsummer—celebrations of renewal and light ([Reddit][3], [realmshelps.net][5])
  * Dawn ceremonies for marriages, births, contracts, and funerals of those not meant for resurrection—all reflecting his ethos of fresh starts and dignified endings ([Reddit][3])
