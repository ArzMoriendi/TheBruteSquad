**To the beneficent members, shield and sword, Sisters and Brothers of the Aster,**

Know ye, far and wide, that our order was decimated by a vile, evil thing hiding in the hinter land near The Crags and we hereby proclaim justice and vengeance and ask all to take up arms.

A contingent of our order conducted a raid on a keep, seeking to destroy the evil within. The creature of the keep, a vile mage of wickedness, released a powerful spell that killed all but our most hardy fighters.

We seek any and all who are of hard hearts and shields to take up our call.  We proclaim this mage a follower of Szass Tam and a member of the Order of Thay.  We beseech our siblings in arms, should you come across a Red Wizard, do not suffer them to live. Seek out evil in all its forms and cleanse the land of its hideousness. 

**In the name of Lathander, may light guide us, and each dawn find us stronger.**

**Lord Justiciar of the Aster**

 **Ercun Haveneye**

**The Dawn is with Us!**
