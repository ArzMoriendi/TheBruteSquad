A mining camp ran by dwarves and humans from Mirabar.  Recently, the leaders in Mirabar lost communication with Hammerfall Run.  This was revealed to be caused by attacks by Goliaths, undead, and a mage.  The mining foreman is the only survivor (Durni Copperpick).

The camp sits on a flat plateau in the foothills of the Spine of the World.  A palisade runs the perimeter of the camp, coming out of the mountainside and moving along the edge of the plateau until meeting up with the mountain again.  There is a guard tower and gate for entry. 

Inside the camp, there is the foreman's quarters, a supply shed, a barracks, a mess hall, a tool shed, a forge and foundry, a tool yard, and a few small other miscellaneous buildings. 

The primary output of the mine is silver.  However, there was a large cave-in around the main area of the mine that will need to be dug out. 

Inside the mine, an ancient dwarven burial chamber was discovered as well as a ritual chamber holding an artifact of legend known as the Stoneheart Reliquary. 

Currently, Hammerfall Run is abandoned and in disrepair due to the attacks of the Goliaths and undead.
