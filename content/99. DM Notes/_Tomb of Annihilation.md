**Tomb of Annihilation** is a Dungeons & Dragons 5th edition adventure module that takes players on a deadly expedition into the heart of Chult, a jungle peninsula filled with perilous terrain, ancient ruins, and horrifying undead. The adventure revolves around the discovery of a curse that causes the souls of the dead to be obliterated, with the characters tasked with finding and stopping the source.

Here's a breakdown of the major points of the adventure:

---

### **Beginning: The Curse of Death**

- **The Death Curse**: The adventure kicks off with a mysterious and deadly curse affecting the world. The curse causes those who have been resurrected (via magic like _Raise Dead_ or _Resurrection_) to wither away and die. Furthermore, those who are currently dead cannot be brought back, as their souls are obliterated. This death curse is spreading across the world, and adventurers are tasked with investigating its source.
    
- **Call to Action**: The characters are hired by a noble named **Jobal** or **Syndra Silvane** (depending on your DM's preference) in the city of **Port Nyanzaru**, to find out the source of this curse and put an end to it. They must venture into the jungles of Chult, where the curse is emanating from a place known as the **Tomb of the Nine Gods**.
    

---

### **Major Cities and Locations**

1. **Port Nyanzaru**
    
    - The main hub and starting point for adventurers in **Tomb of Annihilation**. Port Nyanzaru is a bustling trade city, a melting pot of cultures, and the base of operations for anyone exploring Chult. The city is ruled by a council of merchant princes and is surrounded by perilous jungle. It is also home to dinosaurs, which are used as mounts, and serves as the primary source of information, supplies, and quests for adventurers.
        
2. **The Jungles of Chult**
    
    - The jungle itself is vast, dense, and filled with dangerous wildlife, undead, and other supernatural threats. As players venture through the jungle, they’ll come across:
        
        - **Lost cities** like **Omu** (the city of the Nine Gods, where the tomb is located).
            
        - **Ruins** filled with deadly traps, hostile creatures, and treasures.
            
        - **Encounters** with local tribes, rival adventurers, and dinosaurs.
            
3. **Fort Beluarian**
    
    - A fortified outpost on the border of Chult. It serves as an important base for adventurers looking to venture deeper into the jungle. It also acts as a point of resupply and shelter.
        
4. **Camp Vengeance and Camp Righteous**
    
    - These are military outposts located within the jungle, but both have fallen to monstrous threats. The ruins of these camps offer more side quests for the adventurers.
        

---

### **Major Plot Points**

1. **The Source of the Curse: The Soulmonger**
    
    - The players eventually discover that the source of the Death Curse is the **Soulmonger**, an artifact created by the powerful undead god **Ubtao** and kept in a ruined city. This Soulmonger is responsible for the obliteration of souls and is connected to the **Tomb of the Nine Gods**.
        
2. **The Nine Gods**
    
    - The Tomb of the Nine Gods was created by a group of gods who were once worshipped by the people of Omu, a city that once thrived in Chult. These gods were powerful but also vengeful and malevolent. The players must navigate their tomb, solving puzzles and avoiding deadly traps, to uncover the true nature of the curse.
        
3. **The Betrayal of the Yuan-Ti**
    
    - One of the most shocking plot points involves the involvement of the **Yuan-Ti**, a snake-like race of creatures. They secretly seek to use the Soulmonger for their own purposes, and their machinations threaten the adventurers' progress. Some of them might even serve as allies at first, only to betray the party later.
        
4. **Rival Adventurers**
    
    - Throughout their journey, players encounter a rival group of adventurers, called the **Red Wizards of Thay**. These powerful spellcasters seek the Soulmonger for their own dark purposes and act as a foil to the players' efforts. They are a constant thorn in the players' side, and their plots unfold as the adventurers get closer to the tomb.
        

---

### **Final Battle: The Tomb of the Nine Gods**

- **The Tomb of the Nine Gods** is the final dungeon of the adventure. It is a deadly and challenging location, filled with traps, monsters, and puzzles. The adventurers must explore its depths, facing a series of dangerous challenges, including:
    
    - **The Curse of the Tomb**: Traps and trials left by the ancient gods, testing the players' wits and survival skills.
        
    - **The Nine Gods' Guardians**: Each god has a unique guardian, a powerful creature or being that must be overcome in order to advance.
        
- **Final Confrontation with the Soulmonger**: In the depths of the tomb, the adventurers finally confront the **Soulmonger**, a powerful artifact designed to trap and consume the souls of the dead. To stop the curse, they must destroy the artifact. However, this is no easy task, as they must battle the **Tomb's Final Guardian**, a being of immense power.
- The final battle of **Tomb of Annihilation** leads the players to an intense and deadly confrontation with the **Soulmonger**, a powerful artifact that feeds the essence of an **Atropal**, an undead titan created from the remains of a dead god. This Atropal lies at the heart of the tomb, its existence tied to an ancient and devastating curse known as the **Death Curse**.

### **The True Source of the Death Curse: The Atropal**

The **Soulmonger** itself is revealed to be a **feeding device** for the Atropal, an enormous undead creature born from the edge of the **Negative Energy Plane**, discovered by the archlich **Acererak**. The Atropal was once an unfinished god, an entity left to rot in the cosmic void between life and death. Acererak, ever the schemer, found the Atropal at the edge of the Negative Energy Plane and saw an opportunity to harness its power. He created the Soulmonger to **siphon souls** into the Atropal, using their energy to nourish and grow this titanic undead being.

### **The Archlich Acererak: The True Big Bad**

The true mastermind behind the Death Curse and the creation of the Soulmonger is **Acererak**, the infamous archlich. Acererak's goal is to use the **Atropal** as a vessel for his dark ambitions, draining the life force from the world to raise this undead titan into a **godlike entity**, capable of spreading the death of all things. By feeding it the souls of the dead, the Soulmonger is constantly growing the Atropal's power, making it a monstrous force on the verge of awakening.

As the adventurers journey through the Tomb of the Nine Gods, they uncover Acererak's cruel plans. The archlich’s essence permeates the tomb—his traps, his undead minions, and his terrifying machinations lead the players to understand that he is not only the architect of the tomb but the orchestrator of this catastrophe. His ultimate goal is to use the **Atropal's power** to bring about a new age, one ruled by death and destruction.
