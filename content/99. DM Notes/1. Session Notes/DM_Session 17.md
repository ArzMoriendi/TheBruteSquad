### Scene Setter
 

**CHES 24**  
When last we left our adventurers... 

The Brute Squad stood victorious within the ritual chamber deep beneath Hammerfall Run, where shattered goliath zombies and the frozen remains of their enemies marked the cost of battle. They had broken the will of a Thayan mage, survived deadly magic, and claimed a strange hovering relic of stone and ruby that pulsed with ancient power. With no safer refuge to be found, the party rested within the chamber itself. However, sleep brought no peace. Veldar felt the strengthening presence of the Beast once more. Zwargie was praised and empowered by the dark voice that continued to guide him. GhoulMan was tormented by visions of his murdered family and awoke with vengeance burning hotter than ever. Only Leyawiin rose uncertain, no longer convinced that the path of the Bladesinger was truly hers.

Leaving Hammerfall Run behind, the party emerged to find the mountain in the midst of a tempestuous blizzard. Their first attempt to descend was driven back by wind and ice, forcing them to wait until the storm relented. Even then, the mountain was not finished with them. A sudden partial avalanche battered Leyawiin, Veldar, and Bronn before the group could regroup and press onward. At last, through thinning snow and dawn light, the great city of Mirabar appeared ahead. Escorted inside by Captain Durnan Kraghelm, our adventurers were brought once more before Justiciar Geldryn Axebinder, where their deeds were confirmed and Veldar was fully cleared of all suspicion.

When the party presented the relic, Geldryn revealed its true identity as the legendary Stoneheart Reliquary, a dwarven artifact capable of transforming common ore into its purest form. Understanding the importance of what they had recovered, the Brute Squad entrusted it to Mirabar’s care and were richly rewarded with gold, a Bag of Holding, honored status within the city, access to its libraries, and future use of its portal chamber. In the halls of learning, Leyawiin found clarity at last under the stern guidance of Harbek Bronzewarden, abandoning Bladesinging to pursue the path of Evocation. With gear repaired, supplies gathered, and warm beds finally waiting, the Brute Squad rested in comfort as proven heroes of Mirabar. With roads, seas, and portals now open before them, one question remained: Where would they go next?
