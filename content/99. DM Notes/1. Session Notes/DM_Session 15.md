### Scene Setter
 
**CHES 21**

Our session picks up with The Brute Squad preparing to enter the recently opened section inside the mines of Hammerfall Run. They successfully solved the puzzle to cause the doors to open and were reviewing how best to move forward when an anomaly distracted them.

A rift opened in the cavern room, tearing through the plane, and a large, humanoid entity came through. As he stood up, the party saw a dirty tiefling man who called himself GhoulMan. He described how he was running from a war in his home territory and he had lost all the people he loved and an unknown magic user caused him to fall through the rift.

After a short discussion, the party invited GhoulMan to join them on their current adventure and gave him a quick rundown of what was happening. 

Through tremorsense, Bronn reaffirms that there are 4 entities inside the chamber that could be sensed. 

The party prepared themselves and decided to draw the entities into their chamber. Zwarg walked up to the entry way and shouted “C’mon, come to my cave, Baddies! C’mon come to my cave, ghouls ghouls!” (or something like that). This attracted the attention of the two closest entities which were soon revealed to be Goliath Zombies.

Veldar successful landed a blow with "Thumpy" on the first Goliath Zombie, toppling him. GhoulMan ran past the Zombies into the chamber. The second Goliath Zombie attacked Sarhice, dealing damage. Leyawiin and Bronn attempted magical attacks, dealing damage to both Zombies. Veldar ultimately took out one of the Zombies with a taint attack, after Sarhice had reduced the Zombie to 1 hit point (undead fortitude)

The chamber beyond was a well hewn stone chamber, hexagonal on shape with a raised dais in the centwr and carved, natural stone pillars evenly spaced around. On the dais hovered a large stone, about the size of a human head, blending scintillating granite and a deep ruby-red, multifaceted gem stone. Looking at it from certain angles the stone appeared to beat. 

In the chamber, GhoulMan encountered a living Goliath and a Thayan mage. The Goliath stood to the right of the dais as he entered, and the mage stood in the dais, arms outstretched towards the hovering stone, murdering and changing words unknown. GhoulMan engaged the Goliath who ultimately deflected his attacks. The mage paused long enough to turn and cast a spell (Suggestion) on GhoulMan, turning him against the party with the phrase "Kill your friends". As the party finished dealing with the second Zombie, they were assailed by the Goliath and the charmed GhoulMan.  

The party began to enter the chamber. The mage turned toward Zwarg, recognizing him and said, "You! You were the one that took my relic from my tower. You know not what you carry. You broke my wards and doomed your order. You have caused this."

As Veldar enters the chamber, the Goliath recognizes him and says, with a haughty smile, "Frostfang… your kin would be proud to see what we’ve built. The Ironclaw shall never die!"

The fight ramped up with the Goliath using Thunderwave, her great club, and Frostbite. Leyawiin used magic missile, and attempted to use toll the dead, Bronn helped with healing and guiding bolt. Veldar charged forward to engage the other Goliath.  

Zwarg compelled duel the Thayan mage, causing her to focus her attention on Zwargie. Through his actions, Zwarg caused the mage to lose concentration on GhoulMan, releasing him from his charms.  

GhoulMan reentered the fray, helping to fight the Goliath and remaining Zombie. The party dispatched the Zombie, while the magic users and Zwarg attempted to take out the mage. She used Cone of Cold, freezing Veldar and the Goliath. Leyawiin hit her with magic missile, doing decent damage. The mage chose to teleport away from the fight. 

Upon departing, the room turned dark and still. Using light and the light from Sarhice's blade, they took stock of the situation. Through a combination of firebolt, torch fire, and flammable zombie remains, the party began to thaw Veldar. Slowly. Sarhice attempted to look through the other frozen Goliath's clothes and the pocket broke away.  

Leyawiin and Bronn inspected the hovering stone and gem up close. They learned it had transmutation magical properties and has been touched by necromancy. 

The party chose to end there in the quiet of the ritual chamber
