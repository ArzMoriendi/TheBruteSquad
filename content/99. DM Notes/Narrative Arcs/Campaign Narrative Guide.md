**Terrain:** Foothills of the Spine of the World; broken dwarven roads, frozen streams, dense pine forests.

**Skill/Ability Checks:**

- _Survival DC 14_ → stay on course, avoid avalanche zones.
    
- _Athletics DC 12_ → climb frozen paths.
    
- _Perception DC 13_ → spot ambush signs.
    
- _Arcana DC 15_ → sense lingering necromancy from Zulkassra.
    

**Weather Table (d6):**

1. Clear, biting cold.

5. Starry night with aurora.
    
6. Ice storm, travel halved.
