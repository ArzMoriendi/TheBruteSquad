## **Plot Option 2: “Storm in the Harbor”**

* She offers them a deal: **complete one of three tasks** to help tip the scales back in Suljack’s favor. In return, they’ll get **double the original reward** and a favor owed from Ship Suljack — valuable in Luskan.

---

### **Mission Options (Party Chooses One):**

#### **Option A: Steal the Manifest**

* **Target**: A courier carrying fabricated documents proving Ship Rethnor’s “claim” over Suljack’s cargo.
* **Goal**: Intercept the courier and retrieve (or destroy) the papers before they reach the Arcane Brotherhood.
* **Challenges**: Tail a courier through Luskan's lower docks, sneak into a guarded inn or office, deal with spies or hired mercs.
* **Tone**: Espionage and stealth.

#### **Option B: Sabotage the Seized Ship**

* **Target**: Suljack’s own ship, now docked in Ship Rethnor’s controlled harbor, renamed and being loaded for departure.
* **Goal**: Sneak aboard and sabotage it (set it adrift, scuttle it, or steal back a unique magical compass to prove ownership).
* **Challenges**: Avoid or fight guards, survive an escape route through the harbor, possibly underwater or on a stolen dinghy.
* **Tone**: Infiltration with potential for chaos.

#### **Option C: Blackmail the Rival**

* **Target**: A Ship Rethnor officer with a **dangerous secret** (e.g., deals with pirates from the Trackless Sea or smuggling illicit magic).
* **Goal**: Investigate, gather proof, and threaten to reveal it to the Brotherhood or publicize it.
* **Challenges**: Investigation, social encounters, dealing with criminals, moral ambiguity.
* **Tone**: Roleplay and intrigue.

### **Resolution:**

* Succeed, and Ship Suljack gains leverage, releases the reward, and may even invite the party into future dealings.

## 🕯️ **Secret Option E: “A Knife in the Storm”** *(Locked Unless Persuaded or Intimidated)*

### **Summary:**

There’s a **traitor inside Ship Suljack** — someone helping Ship Rethnor and sabotaging internal operations. Talurra suspects who it is… but has **no hard proof** and no political capital to move against them.

She offers the party an under-the-table job: **eliminate the suspected traitor quietly**, plant forged evidence on the body, and make it look like a **Rethnor assassination** — turning public perception **against Ship Rethnor** and freeing Suljack’s coffers *and* political hand.

---

### **How It Unlocks:**

If the party pushes with a **DC 16–18 Persuasion** (appeal to Suljack’s desperation or offer to take on *real* work)
OR
**DC 15–17 Intimidation** (e.g., “We didn’t walk into Luskan to chase couriers — give us the job you’re scared to do”),
then Talurra leans in close and mutters:

> “There *is* another way… but it ain’t clean, and it sure as the sea ain’t official. You do this for me — no questions, no names — and I’ll see to it that Suljack pays you double, and owes you one.”

---

### **Mission Objective:**

* **Track, confirm, and eliminate** the suspected traitor: **Bosun Drevik Salt**, a respected Suljack officer working secretly with Ship Rethnor.
* **Plant fabricated documents** on his corpse that make it appear he was **working as a double agent for Rethnor**, then dump the body publicly — or discreetly, depending on party tactics.
* Create a political narrative that **frames Ship Rethnor**, lifting the embargo.

---

### **Location:**

* **The Sloshed Siren Tavern**, a neutral ground favored by ship crews — noisy, smoky, hard to draw attention.
* Drevik meets contacts here in secret, or retreats to his **modest sea-captain’s quarters** at the edge of Suljack territory.

### **Challenges:**

* **Tracking and tailing Drevik** without alerting him (Skill challenge?).

* Optional twist: **Drevik is guilty — but only partly**. The full traitor may be someone else.

### **Enemies / Obstacles:**
