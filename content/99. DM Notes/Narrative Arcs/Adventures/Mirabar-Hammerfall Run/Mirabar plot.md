# 🗺️ **Quest: Ashes of the Shattered Peaks**

*A level 3 quest for 6 players, set near Mirabar — connecting Veldar's and Zwargie’s backstories, and introducing Red Wizard interference ahead of *Tomb of Annihilation**\*

---

## 🎯 Overview

The party is hired by a dwarven envoy of the Marchion of Mirabar to investigate a **silent mining outpost** in the foothills. A surviving miner’s message spoke of **giant-sized attackers**, **arcane darkness**, and a **black-robed woman** searching for a dwarven relic.

Behind this is **Zulkassra Vlorrenth**, a Red Wizard of Thay, and **Tharna Ironclaw**, a war shaman of the Goliath clan that massacred Veldar's tribe. They seek the **Stoneheart Reliquary**, a dwarven soul-binding relic that Zulkassra intends to repurpose for necromantic resurrection magic.

Zulkassra recognizes **Zwargie** as the paladin who stole a different artifact from her tower — the act that **unwittingly unleashed an explosion** that destroyed his order.

---

## 🧩 Adventure Structure

### 🏰 Act 1 – A Quiet Favor from the Marchion

#### Scene: *Council Chambers, Mirabar*

The party is summoned to speak with **Geldryn Axebinder**, a dwarven councilor and envoy of the Marchion of Mirabar. He briefs them in a private chamber of the **Hall of Sparkling Stones**.

> **Geldryn Axebinder’s Hook:**
> “One of our outposts — Hammerfall Run — went dark three days ago. The runners we sent didn’t return. The last message mentioned... shadows, large raiders, and some kind of dark sorcery. We need discretion. The Council can’t risk public panic or northern trade being disrupted.”

#### Skill Checks

* **Insight (DC 13):** Geldryn is downplaying the danger. He fears a diplomatic scandal — especially if Goliaths or Red Wizards are involved.
* **Investigation (DC 12):** Examining the missive reveals it was burned around the edges by **black fire** — necromantic in origin.
* **Perception (DC 14):** Noticing a **red wax seal** on the message — Thayan in style, nearly scraped off.

**Reward:** 50 gp for completion; bonus if done quietly.

---

### 🥶 Act 2 – The Road to Hammerfall Run

The journey takes a day and a half. The closer they get, the colder and more unnaturally silent the land becomes.

#### Encounter: **Ambushed Wagon**

A supply wagon lies overturned. Crates are smashed. The horses are missing.

* Tracks lead off the road — Goliath-sized boot prints, unnatural claw marks.

#### Skill Checks

* **Survival (DC 13):** Identify multiple **Goliath-sized tracks**, and strange humanoid tracks that don’t breathe (undead).
* **Investigation (DC 15):** Find **burned black dwarven shields** and a torn red cloth with **arcane glyphs**.
* **Arcana (DC 12):** Recognize the glyphs as **Thayan protective sigils** — normally used in necromantic wards.

#### Combat:

* 2x **Undead Dwarf Skeletons**
* 1x **Ironclaw Goliath Berserker** (CR 1)

---

### ⛏️ Act 3 – The Mines of Hammerfall Run

The entrance to the mine is eerily quiet. Smoke and ash mar the stone. Inside: dead miners, collapsed tunnels, strange glowing runes along the rock.

#### Exploration

* **Perception (DC 12):** Hear muffled sobbing from a storage room — it's **Foreman Durni Copperpick**, terrified and dehydrated.
* **Insight (DC 12):** Durni is holding back — he saw a woman in red robes speaking to a Goliath war-leader, discussing a "heart-stone beneath the vault."

> **Durni’s Quote:**
> “They weren’t here to rob us. They were... looking for something. Said it was forged by the ancestors. Called it the *Stoneheart*.”

---

### 💀 Act 4 – The Vault and the Confrontation

The mine leads deeper to a **collapsed stone vault**. Necrotic energy pulses from within. Bones have been disturbed — dwarves buried long ago.

#### The Final Chamber

Zulkassra and Tharna are in the final room, mid-ritual. The **Stoneheart Reliquary**, a massive spherical dwarven relic, is set on a dais. Black fire etches the floor.

#### Confrontation

Zulkassra recognizes **Zwargie** at once.

> “You... *you’re alive?* I felt your hand on my wards when you stole the sphere. You thought it a holy relic — but it was a keystone. You *shattered* a containment web holding a dozen bound souls. Your order died by your hand.”

**Veldar** may recognize Tharna by name or tattoos — she was present on the Night of the Shattered Peaks.

> Tharna to Veldar:
> “Look what we’ve become, Frostfang. You serve pink-skins in temples. I rebuild a clan. Soon, the Ironclaw will never die.”

#### Combat:

**Boss Fight**

* **Zulkassra Vlorrenth** (Red Wizard necromancer – CR 4)
* **Tharna Ironclaw** (Barbarian-druid war shaman – CR 2)
* 2x **Skeletons** or **Undead Ironclaw Raiders** (CR 1/4)

## 📜 Stat Blocks

---

### 🧠 **Zulkassra Vlorrenth** – Human Red Wizard of Thay (CR 4)

**Medium humanoid (human), lawful evil**
**AC:** 13 (Mage Armor)
**HP:** 65 (10d8 + 20)
**Speed:** 30 ft.
**Saves:** Int +6, Wis +4
**Skills:** Arcana +6, Insight +4, Persuasion +4
**Resistances:** Necrotic
**Languages:** Common, Thayan, Draconic

#### Abilities:

* **Grim Knowledge:** Has advantage on saving throws vs undead and necrotic effects.
* **Spellcasting:** 5th-level caster (Intelligence 16, spell save DC 14, +6 to hit)

**Cantrips (at will):** Mage Hand, Ray of Frost, Toll the Dead, Prestidigitation
**1st (4 slots):** Mage Armor, Shield, Magic Missile
**2nd (3 slots):** Mirror Image, Hold Person
**3rd (2 slots):** Animate Dead, Counterspell

#### Actions:

* **Necrotic Ray (Cantrip):** Ranged Spell Attack: +6 to hit, 2d8 necrotic damage.
* **Raise Bone Servants (Recharge 5–6):** Raises up to 2 skeletons or zombies from nearby corpses (as Animate Dead).

#### Reactions:

* **Counterspell** or **Shield**

---

### 🧊 **Tharna Ironclaw** – Goliath Barbarian-War Shaman (CR 2)

**Medium humanoid (goliath), chaotic neutral**
**AC:** 15 (hide + stone endurance)
**HP:** 55 (6d12 + 18)
**Speed:** 30 ft.
**Saving Throws:** Str +5, Con +5
**Skills:** Survival +4, Intimidation +4
**Damage Resistances:** Cold, Necrotic

#### Features:

* **Rage (2/day):** Bonus action; gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing; +2 melee damage.
* **Stone’s Endurance (1/rest):** Reduce damage taken by 1d12+3 as reaction.
* **Druidic Magic:** Wisdom-based, DC 13

**Cantrips:** Druidcraft, Frostbite
**1st (2 slots):** Entangle, Thunderwave
**2nd (1 slot):** Spike Growth

#### Actions:

* **Greatclub:** +5 to hit, 1d8+3 bludgeoning
* **Rending Strike (Recharge 6):** 2 melee attacks + if both hit, target makes DC 13 CON save or is stunned until end of Tharna's next turn.
