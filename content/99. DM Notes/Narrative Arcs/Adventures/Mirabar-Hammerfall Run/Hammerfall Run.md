## Walkthrough by Zone

### 1. **Approach & Perimeter**

- The camp is surrounded by a **timber palisade** (10 ft logs), now half-burned and broken in places.
    
- **Watchtower** (20 ft): Top platform scorched, with a dwarf skeleton slumped against the railing, arrow still clutched in its hand.
    
- **Signs of Attack:** Broken gates, boot prints, claw marks, and patches of unnatural black scorch on the ground.
    

**Clues / Checks**

- _Survival DC 13_: Distinguish goliath prints from undead shambling tracks.
    
- _Arcana DC 12_: Black scorch marks are necrotic fire.
    

---

### 2. **Outer Camp**

- **Barracks:** Beds overturned, bloodstains, shattered dwarven axes. One undead dwarf still animates here unless destroyed earlier.
    
- **Foreman’s Cabin:** Door broken in. Signs of forced entry; desk drawers ransacked. (DC 12 Investigation: hidden drawer with shift rosters, confirming **30 miners, only 5 bodies found**).
    
- **Mess Hall:** Tables overturned; ash piled in corners. Necrotic runes faintly etched into the floor (ritual prep).
    
- **Supply Shed & Storage Barn:** Most supplies gone. Survivors could be hiding here (use for roleplay, rescue, or just clues).
    

---

### 3. **Mine Yard**

- **Tool Yard:** Abandoned pickaxes, sledges, carts. Some covered in frozen blood.
    
- **Smelter Forge:** Fire long-dead, slag scattered. Runes etched into smelter stone — necrotic ritual used forge as focus point to weaken vault seal.
    

---

### 4. **Mine Entrance & Shafts**

- Wide stone arch, dwarven runes overhead (“Toil Brings Wealth, Wealth Brings Strength”). Defaced with **blackened Thayan glyphs**.
    
- Inside: **low smoke and drifting ash**. Walls scarred from both battle and collapse.
    

---

### 5. **Mine Interior Encounters**

- **Collapsed Tunnel with Runes:** A choke point where Zulkassra carved necrotic wards.
    
    - Any attempt to pass triggers **2 Skeleton Miners** bursting from rubble.
        
- **Dead-End Shaft:** Crates of unrefined ore, plus a corpse pinned by a minecart.

    - Desecrated — 2–3 corpses are animated here as **Skeletons or Zombies**.
