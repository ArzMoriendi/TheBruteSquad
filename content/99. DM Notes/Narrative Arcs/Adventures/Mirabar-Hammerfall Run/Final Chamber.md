## Final Chamber: Heart of the Lattice

### **Read-Aloud Description**

*The twisting geometry of the maze opens into a colossal cathedral of fractured crystal. Each surface reflects infinite copies of you in shifting colors. At the center hovers a newborn star, pulsing like a heartbeat, contained within the Lattice’s equations.*

*Sylvie is suspended above it, bound by three radiant chains inscribed with living glyphs. Each chain hums with the resonance of fragments you’ve carried. As you step forward, the shadows of the crystal converge into a single form: a towering silhouette of runes and flame—the Threshold Keeper.*

*Its voice reverberates through the chamber:*
**“Entry denied. Equations complete. Architect—you will not unmake what you built.”**

---

### **The Encounter Structure**

This final sequence is both **puzzle and boss fight**, demanding PCs juggle breaking Sylvie’s chains and surviving the Keeper’s assault.

---

#### 1. **Objective — Break Chains**

Each chain can be broken by completing one of the following:

* **Arcana (DC 18):** Interfacing with the chain’s glyphs to rewrite its equations.
* **Athletics (DC 18):** Physically tearing the chain, empowered by the fragments.
* **Codex Ritual:** If Leyawiin channels the Astral Codex, she can attempt a special ritual. Each ritual attempt requires her action and **Constitution save DC 15** to endure psychic backlash.

**Progress:**

* The party needs **3 total successes** to break all chains (not 3 each).
* Each failure before a success causes the chain to lash out: **2d10 radiant damage** to the attempting PC.

When the **final chain breaks**, Sylvie falls—unconscious but alive—into the arms of whoever is below.

---

#### 2. **Boss — Threshold Keeper**

* **Base:** *Kraken Priest* (VGtM p.215).
* **Reskin:** Its body is a shifting humanoid silhouette made of luminous runes and void-fire.
* **Adjustments:**

  * HP: 180 (20d8+100).
  * Damage Resistances: Psychic, Radiant, Necrotic.
  * Condition Immunities: Charmed, Frightened.

**Legendary Actions (choose 1 at end of another’s turn):**

* **Gravity Shift (Costs 2 Actions):** Inverts gravity in a 30-ft. radius for 1 round (Strength save DC 16 to cling; fail = fall upward, then downward next round for **4d10 force damage**).
* **Mirror Split (Costs 2 Actions):** Creates 1d4 illusory duplicates (function like *Mirror Image*).
* **Psychic Wave (Costs 1 Action):** Emits psychic blast, all PCs within 20 ft. **Intelligence save DC 15**, 2d8 psychic damage and pushed 10 ft. on fail.

**Lair Actions (initiative 20):**

* **Chain Lash:** One radiant chain lashes at a PC, +9 to hit, 3d10 radiant damage, grapple on hit.
* **Crystal Collapse:** A section of floor crumbles into void. PCs in 15-ft. radius must Dex save DC 16 or fall, taking 4d10 force and sliding into precarious ledges.

---

#### 3. **Resolution**

* Once the **chains are broken** and the Keeper reduced to 0 HP, the cathedral destabilizes. The newborn star cracks, flooding the room with radiant energy.
* The Keeper, shattering into runes, turns its attention to Agatha through the crystal reflections (if she’s present, or via psychic projection if she’s aboard the *Tardisian Blue*):

  * *“Your equations still bind the living, Architect. You may flee—but the Lattice remembers.”*

**Sylvie:**

* Collapses, unconscious but alive. Her chains leave faint star-shaped scars on her wrists and neck.
* She whispers, barely audible: *“…thank you…”*

---

#### 4. **Escape Sequence**

* As the chamber collapses, the party must **activate the Astral Codex**.
* **Arcana DC 15** or **Use of Gate (PHB p.244)** will stabilize a portal.
* Failure on the first round → collapse escalates: each round, **Dex save DC 15 or 3d10 falling crystal damage**.
* After **3 rounds**, the cathedral implodes, and any PC not through the portal is erased from existence.

---

### **Narrative Prompts for Roleplay**

* **Before Combat:**

  * The Keeper: *“Sylvie’s fate is sealed. The Architect has no claim here.”*
  * Sylvie’s muffled cries: *“Please… it hurts…”*
* **During Combat:**

  * Keeper taunts each success: *“Your strength cannot unmake the law.”*
  * At 50% HP: *“The Lattice bends. Impossible!”*
* **After Sylvie’s Rescue:**

  * Agatha feels the weight of responsibility: the Keeper’s words confirm her role as co-creator of the prison.
  * PCs may question her—was she complicit, or deceived by greater powers?

---

✅ **Victory:** Sylvie rescued, party escapes with Codex intact, Lattice destabilized.
❌ **Failure:** If PCs fall or Codex fails, they are consumed by the collapsing construct—the campaign ends with their erasure.
