## ✅ SESSION PREP PROCESS (Outline)

### 🧭 Step 1: Narrative Planning – The Gate Scene

#### Key Objectives:

- Highlight Mirabar’s tight security and rising tension
    
- Plant distrust of **Veldar** due to Goliath/Ironclaw rumors
    
- Offer two outcomes: diplomacy or detainment
    

#### Tools:

- **Persuasion DC**: 16 (due to prejudice and heightened alert)
    
- Allow **Insight** to learn why suspicion is high (DC 12: "recent Ironclaw raids")
    
- Success → escorted under guard to the **Hall of Sparkling Stones**
    
- Failure → Veldar arrested, but party may negotiate his release later via Geldryn
    

---

### 🛡 Step 2: Guard Encounter Stat Blocks

Use these NPCs as flexible components of both:

- Gate encounter (investigation, roleplay)

### 🧔‍♂️ **Captain Durnan Kraghelm** (Dwarf Veteran Commander)

**Medium humanoid (dwarf), lawful neutral**  
**AC** 17 (chain mail + shield)  
**HP** 84 (13d8 + 26)  
**Speed** 25 ft.  
**Str** 16 | **Dex** 11 | **Con** 15 | **Int** 10 | **Wis** 13 | **Cha** 14

**Saving Throws**: Str +5, Wis +4  
**Skills**: Insight +4, Intimidation +5, Perception +4  
**Damage Resistances**: Poison  
**Languages**: Common, Dwarvish

[[Durnan Kraghelm Prompts]]

**Abilities**:

- **Dwarven Resilience**: Advantage on saves vs. poison
    
- **Command Presence** _(1/rest)_: All allies within 30 ft. gain +1 to attack rolls and saving throws for 1 minute
    

**Actions**:

- **Multiattack**: 2 melee attacks
    
- **Warhammer**: +5 to hit, 1d8+3 bludgeoning (1d10+3 two-handed)
    
- **Commanding Strike** _(Recharge 5–6)_: One ally within 30 ft. may make an attack as a reaction

## 🎲 Step 3: Social Encounter Tools

Give the party opportunity to:

|Action|Outcome|
|---|---|
|**Persuade (DC 16)**|Gain access with escort|
|**Insight (DC 12)**|Guards fear recent Goliath incursions|
|**History (DC 10)**|Mirabar has past conflict with Ironclaw clan|
|**Deception/Disguise**|Veldar may attempt to hide identity (DC 15)|
|**Combat**|Overwhelming force; non-lethal takedown; possible imprisonment|

Escalating guard reinforcements: 1 captain, 4 guards → 12 total if combat continues.
