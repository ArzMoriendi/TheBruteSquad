# **Bronn’s Character Arc – “Shadows of the Past” (Shar Edition)**

**Overview:**
Bronn’s arc focuses on his unresolved grief, loyalty to Kelemvor, and temptation by a hidden sect of Shar worshippers. The arc unfolds organically alongside *Tomb of Annihilation* but remains independent of the main plot.

---

## **Phase 1 – Neverwinter: Whisper of the Past**

**Trigger:** The party arrives in Neverwinter after leaving Mirabar.

**Key NPC:** **Edran**, former associate of Bronn’s parents, knows of their secret Shar worship.

**Hook/Roleplay Prompt:**

* Edran notices Bronn’s resemblance to his parents.

  > “I’m surprised you follow Kelemvor after all the research your parents did. You look so much like them… strong, clever… like they were.”
* He hands Bronn a small **token of Shar**: a polished obsidian ring with a crescent moon in blackened silver, etched with faint shadowy runes.

  > “Your parents would want you to have this. Wear it in their memory.”

**Skill Checks & Roleplay Options:**

* **Insight DC 16:** Determine if Edran is trustworthy or has ulterior motives.
* **Investigation / Religion DC 15:** Examine the token and the writings found in a small study Edran points to, revealing journals in his parents’ handwriting praising Shar’s philosophy.
* **Persuasion / Deception DC 14:** Navigate Edran’s subtle attempts to tempt Bronn without fully committing.

**Roleplay Guidance:**

* Journals present Shar as altruistic, the provider of guidance, Kelemvor as rigid or distant.
* Edran frames involvement as “discovering your parents’ legacy” rather than worship.
* The whispers from Shar subtly begin now, suggesting insight, guidance, or subtle nudges in decisions.

**Outcome:**

* Bronn is now aware of his parents’ secret faith.
* Early moral tension begins; his faith in Kelemvor is questioned but not broken.

---

## **Phase 2 – Port Nyanzaru: Temptation through Indulgence**

**Trigger:** Arrival in Port Nyanzaru.

**Hook:**

* The **Shar sect** in Port Nyanzaru has influence over dinosaur racing and gambling circuits.
* Bronn’s gambling and drinking tendencies are tested through these events, offering both temptation and rewards.

**Skill Checks & Mechanics:**

* **Arcana / Religion DC 15:** Recognize subtle magical manipulation or ritualistic influence in the races.
* **Insight DC 17:** Detect sect members trying to manipulate him socially or spiritually.
* **Wisdom Saving Throw DC 16:** Resist compulsions to overindulge or be lured into shady deals.
* **Performance / Sleight of Hand DC 14:** Participate in gambling without losing too much or appearing compromised.

**Roleplay Prompts:**

* Sect members praise his “luck and insight,” subtly connecting his skill to his parents’ teachings.
* Whispered suggestions from Shar grow more insistent, highlighting possible rewards and personal satisfaction.

**Outcome:**

* Bronn either gains experience resisting temptation or begins to waver under Shar’s influence.
* Seeds are planted for later moral choices.

---

## **Phase 3 – Chult: Trial by Faith (False Hydra Scenario)**

**Trigger:** Party encounters a village terrorized by a false hydra.

**Purpose:** Test Bronn’s ability to rely on Kelemvor versus Shar.

**Skill Checks & Mechanics:**

* **Perception / Insight DC 16:** Detect the presence and patterns of the false hydra.
* **Religion DC 15:** Channel divine guidance from Kelemvor or Shar during encounters.
* **Attack / Spell DC:** Decision-driven mechanics: He can choose powers that reflect Kelemvor’s mercy or Shar’s darker, pragmatic methods.

**Roleplay Prompts:**

* Bronn must decide whether to **protect innocents at personal risk** or exploit the situation for efficiency and power (as Shar might suggest).
* Conversations with the party can highlight his internal conflict.

**Outcome:**

* Bronn’s faith and moral compass are further clarified.
* The experience strengthens his conviction in one path and sets the stage for the final phase.

---

## **Phase 4 – Crisis of Faith: The Rite of Shadows (Priestess Thalira)**

**Trigger:** Sect culminates in a ritual to **elevate Shar’s influence in Chult**.

**Key NPCs:**

* **High Priestess Thalira:** Charismatic, authoritative, and the true leader of the Shar sect.
* **Role of Edran:** Introduced earlier as the link to Bronn’s parents, not present here.

**Hook/Roleplay Prompt:**

> Thalira: “Bronn, you are ready to lead this rite. Knowledge, power, the chance to commune with your parents… it is all within your reach.”

**Promises:**

* Enhanced insight and shadow-based powers.
* Indulgence in gambling, drink, and worldly pleasure.
* Communion with deceased parents (illusory or controlled by Shar).

**Hidden Consequences:**

* Physical, spiritual, and moral degradation; becomes a husk if fully submitted.
* Permanent loss of favor with Kelemvor.

**Skill Checks & Mechanics:**

* **Religion / Arcana DC 16:** Sense the true nature of the ritual.
* **Wisdom Saving Throw DC 16:** Resist full submission to Shar.
* **Insight DC 18:** Recognize Thalira’s promises are manipulative.
* **Charisma / Persuasion DC 15:** Attempt to negotiate or disrupt the rite.

**Roleplay Prompts:**

* Thalira frames the rite as honoring his parents’ legacy.
* The token from Edran glows subtly, representing Shar’s influence during key choices.
* Bronn experiences vivid visions of his parents, morally compelling him to participate—but the visions are fabricated by Shar’s divine manipulation.

**Outcomes:**

* **Decline / resist rite:** Bronn reaffirms loyalty to Kelemvor, retaining agency and faith.
* **Participate fully:** Gains temporary power and indulgence, permanently loses favor with Kelemvor, and becomes a vessel for Shar’s will.
