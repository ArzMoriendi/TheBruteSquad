## ⚔️ HOW THE QUEST STARTS

The quest can begin in **any of the following ways**:

### 1. **The Necklace Hook** *(Organic Discovery)*

While in the jungle (e.g., between Yellyark and Mbala), the party finds a **mortally wounded Chultan man** (use **Commoner**, MM p. 345). He whispers:

> “My sister… I was visiting her. The village… it’s gone. No one believes me. I have her necklace. She was *real*.”

He dies clutching a simple wooden necklace — **hand-carved in the shape of a Chultan tree-frog**, recognizable as a common village talisman from central Chult. It radiates **faint necromantic residue** (*Detect Magic*, PHB p. 231).

---

### 2. **Merchant Prince Hook**

In Port Nyanzaru, **Merchant Prince Kwayothé** (from *Tomb of Annihilation*, p. 28) asks the party to investigate **missing trade goods** and **caravans** that were meant to pass through a small jungle village called **Shilku Ridge** — now completely **absent from maps and memory**.

She’s more concerned with **recovering stolen cargo**, but offers gold for confirming what happened to the village.

---

### 3. **Tribal Leader Hook**

A local Chultan leader — such as **Chief Yokka** (ToA p. 90, from the village of Ataaz Muhahah) — asks the party to investigate why **ancestral spirits** no longer respond during funeral rites. They believe **"something has eaten the names of the dead."**

## 🧩 PART 1: “The Forgotten Place”

The party navigates into a dense, mist-shrouded area of the jungle (ToA travel rules, p. 38), following tracks, rumors, or pure chance. They discover:

* A small **abandoned village**, half-sunken into the jungle
* **Signs of hasty burial**: shallow graves, open tombs, forgotten shrines
* **No living inhabitants**, but clear signs people once lived here
* A **headless body** in Kelemvorite vestments (religion check DC 13 to identify)

> Bronn hears a faint whisper at night:
> “You never came. We were forgotten.”

After a night in or near the ruined village, the party experiences the effects of the **False Hydra’s song**.

### Mechanics: The Song (False Hydra)

> *"You cannot fight what you cannot remember."*

**Effect**: Any creature within **1 mile** of the False Hydra must make a **DC 16 Wisdom saving throw** every hour or:

* Be unable to perceive the False Hydra (even if seen)
* Forget all prior interactions with the Hydra or its victims
* Be unable to speak about the Hydra or remember its name

**Mechanics Used**:

* *Modify Memory* (PHB p. 261), reflavored as a lair-wide effect
* *Sanctuary* (PHB p. 272): Used narratively for Hydra “protection” while feeding

## 🧬 TRAITS

### 🧠 **False Song** (Aura, 1 mile)

Any creature within 1 mile that can hear the False Hydra must make a **DC 16 Wisdom saving throw** every hour. On a failed save:

* The creature **cannot perceive or recall** the False Hydra.
* It **forgets** any creature the False Hydra has devoured, as if they never existed.
* This effect persists until the creature:

  * Succeeds the save,
  * Leaves the radius for more than 24 hours,
  * Is affected by *Greater Restoration*, *Dispel Magic* (DC 16), *Silence*, or *Protection from Evil and Good*.

> **Spell Reference**: *Modify Memory*, PHB p. 261

---

### 🧠 **Many-Mouthed Horror**

The False Hydra begins with **3 heads**, gaining one head for every 2 humanoids it has devoured (up to 7).
Each head grants an **additional reaction**, and **Multiattack** can target that many creatures.
If the Hydra takes **25 damage** in a single turn, one head may be severed.

> Treat like Hydra heads (MM p. 190), but psychic and necrotic-themed instead of elemental.

---

### 🕳️ **Burrowing Terror**

The False Hydra can **burrow through unworked earth and stone**, leaving behind tunnels large enough for Large creatures. It can **emerge** from any burrow as part of a movement or attack.

---

### 🔮 **Incomprehensible Presence**

Creatures who first see the False Hydra (if not affected by False Song) must succeed a **DC 15 Charisma saving throw** or be **stunned until the end of their next turn**.
Success renders the creature immune for 24 hours.

---

## 🗡️ ACTIONS

### 👄 Multiattack

The False Hydra makes one **Bite attack per head**.

---

### 🧠 Bite

*Melee Weapon Attack:* +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target
*Hit:* 18 (3d8 + 5) piercing damage + 9 (2d8) psychic damage
If the target is a creature, it must succeed a **DC 16 Constitution saving throw** or lose a **short-term memory** — determined by the DM (last turn, the round, or an NPC’s name).

> Spell Reference: Memory erasure effect flavored from *Modify Memory* (PHB p. 261)

---

### 🌫️ Consume Identity (Recharge 5–6)

The False Hydra targets one humanoid corpse within reach that died within the last 24 hours.

* It devours the corpse in 1 round.
* All non-magical **evidence of that creature’s existence disappears** within 1 minute: notes, names, tokens, records.
* All creatures within 1 mile must succeed a DC 16 Wis save or forget the person entirely.

---

## ⚡ LAIR ACTIONS *(Optional)*

If fought in its **burrow lair**, on initiative count 20 (losing ties), the False Hydra can take one of the following lair actions:

### 🧠 Psychic Suppression

Each creature of the Hydra’s choice in the lair must make a **DC 15 Intelligence saving throw** or forget what they were doing. They lose concentration and suffer **disadvantage on their next attack roll**.

---

### 🎭 Faceless Friend

A False Hydra head mimics the voice or face of someone the PC once knew (no save required). Each PC must succeed a **DC 14 Wisdom save** or be **frightened** for 1 round.

---

## 🔁 LEGENDARY ACTIONS

The False Hydra can take **3 legendary actions**, choosing from the options below. Only one can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. It regains all legendary actions at the start of its turn.

### 👁️ Lurk (Costs 1 Action)

The Hydra sinks into the earth and gains **half cover** until the start of its next turn.

---

### 🧠 Echo of the Eaten (Costs 2 Actions)

The Hydra emits a psychic moan. Each creature within 30 ft. that can hear it must succeed on a **DC 16 Wisdom save** or lose **1 prepared spell slot** (random, lowest level available).

---

### 💀 Bite Again (Costs 2 Actions)

Makes a single Bite attack.
