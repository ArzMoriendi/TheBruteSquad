# ❄️ Frost Does Not Forgive

*“Blood is thicker than ice—but ice remembers.”*

### Type: Side Arc (2–3 Sessions)

### Ideal Levels: 6–10

### Location: Frost-scarred ruins in Northern Chult

### Ties To: **Tomb of Annihilation**, **Red Wizards of Thay**, **The Ring of Winter**

---

## 🌨️ BACKGROUND

After defeating **Zulkassra Vlorrenth** and **Thorna Ironclaw**, the party continues their journey through Chult. Rumors begin to reach Port Nyanzaru of a **strange winter spreading in the jungle**—localized zones of frostbite, frozen corpses, and chilling howls from creatures "too large to be human."

These reports point to a **ragtag band of Goliaths**, calling themselves the **Cold Hearts**, operating deep in the jungle.

Among them is **Yrga Flintcaller**, a mysterious Goliath woman who claims she survived the annihilation of her clan, the **Stonevein**. But Yrga is lying. She is **Ironclaw**, loyal to the same war clan that destroyed Veldar’s tribe—and she is now **secretly working with the Red Wizards of Thay**, aiding them in their hunt for the **Ring of Winter**.

---

## 🧊 THE CORE THEMES

* **Identity & Loyalty**: Veldar must question who he is without his tribe and whether his strength is enough to define a new legacy.
* **Manipulation**: Yrga offers a family—but under false pretenses.
* **Cold Ambition**: The Red Wizards remain hidden, but Yrga is their agent, using brute force to clear the jungle for their purposes.
* **The Ring of Winter's Corruption**: Not physically present, but its effects influence terrain and draw evil toward it.

---

## 🧩 ARC STRUCTURE

---

### 🔹 **Act I: Frozen Signs**

#### Hook Options:

* A **merchant prince** asks the party to investigate a jungle camp flash-frozen overnight.
* A **Chultan guide** tells a chilling tale of white-skinned giants chanting in the night.
* The party stumbles upon a **frozen ruin** while traversing northern Chult.
* A **villager shows Veldar a frost-covered Goliath totem** found on a corpse. The totem is from the Frostfang tribe—Veldar’s own.

#### Key Encounter:

* A **small ruin blanketed in unnatural frost**, temperature dropping to −30°F.
  *(Source: Ring of Winter Lore — FR Campaign Setting / Tomb of Annihilation)*

* Creatures:

  * **1x Coldlight Walker** (*Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes*, p. 210)
  * **3–5x Ice Mephits** (*Monster Manual*, p. 215)
  * **Signs of powerful cold magic** like *Wall of Ice*, *Cone of Cold*, *Sleet Storm* (*PHB*)

* Veldar finds a **weathered Frostfang Totem** embedded in a glacier heartstone—an emotional trigger tied to his clan.

---

### 🔹 **Act II: The Cold Hearts**

* Deep in the frost-choked jungle, the party locates a nomadic Goliath camp. These are the **Cold Hearts**, survivors of destroyed clans—including scattered Ironclaws, Frostfang remnants, and others.

* The group is led by **Yrga Flintcaller**, who claims to be from the **Stonevein Clan**—a lie. She’s **Ironclaw**, and she alone knows she’s **working directly with the Red Wizards of Thay**, who remain unseen.

* Yrga tempts Veldar with **brotherhood and belonging**, spinning tales of his chieftain possibly surviving, lost in exile.

* She offers to make him **a chieftain**, or “First Axe” of a new Goliath order. Her words are honest; her motives are not.

---

### ❄️ Yrga Flintcaller – Overview

* **Race**: Goliath (Ironclaw, posing as Stonevein)
* **Class**: Barbarian / Warlock (flavored pact with Red Wizards)
* **Tactics**: Uses strength, trust, and cold-enhanced items to manipulate Cold Hearts and overwhelm enemies.
* **Goal**: Clear ruins and wilderness to allow Red Wizards to move freely and unearth the Ring of Winter’s last known paths.

> She has **no lieutenants**. Her control is absolute through charisma and fear.

---

### 🔹 **Act III: The Glacier Heart**

The Cold Hearts lead the party to a ruin infused with ancient **frost magic**—not a known Temple of the Ring, but possibly where **Artus Cimber** passed decades ago. The land remembers.

* **Frozen Temple Ruins**: Permanently affected by the Ring’s climate control.
  *Effect: 120 ft radius of −30°F cold (Source: Ring of Winter lore)*

* **Defenses/Creatures**:

  * Ice Mephits
  * Frost-elemental constructs
  * Ghostly echoes (hallucinations of Veldar’s past)

* **The Ritual Site**: A cracked plinth once used by the Red Wizards. Clues show Thayan glyphs, suggesting **Yrga has returned on their behalf**.

## **Special Traits**

### 🧊 **Frozen Heart (Recharge 5–6) [Homebrew]**

As a bonus action, Yrga channels her pact with the Red Wizards, summoning magical frost through her veins. For 1 minute:

* Her melee attacks deal an additional **1d6 cold damage**.
* Creatures that hit her with melee attacks take **1 cold damage**.
* While active, her presence chills the air within 10 ft. Any creature that ends its turn within 10 ft. of her must make a **DC 15 Con save** or have their **movement halved** until the start of their next turn.

> *(Homebrew adaptation; thematically similar to the **Armor of Agathys** and **Spirit Shroud** effects)*

---

### 💢 **Rage (2/Long Rest) [Barbarian]**

As a bonus action, Yrga enters a rage for 1 minute:

* Advantage on Strength checks and saves.
* +2 bonus to melee weapon damage with Strength.
* Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.

---

### 🪓 **Reckless Attack**

Yrga can choose to make her melee weapon attacks with advantage. Attack rolls against her also have advantage until her next turn.

---

### 🧱 **Stone’s Endurance (1/Short Rest)**

When Yrga takes damage, she can use her reaction to roll 1d12 + 3 and reduce the damage by that amount.

---

### 🗻 **Mountain Born (Goliath Trait)**

Yrga is acclimated to high altitude and cold climates and has resistance to cold damage (already incorporated).

---

### 🔮 **Red Wizard Pact (Arcane Adept) [Homebrew + NPC Spellcasting]**

Though not a full caster, Yrga can invoke magic granted by her pact. She casts spells using **Charisma (spell save DC 15)**.

**Innate Spellcasting (Pact Magic):**

* **1/day each**:

  * *Armor of Agathys* (3rd level – 15 temp HP, 15 cold dmg)
  * *Hold Person*
  * *Ice Knife*
  * *Misty Step*
  * *Command*

> *(These are flavored as runes etched into her skin by Red Wizards. Homebrew pact caster similar to “Cult Fanatic” template)*

## **Actions**

### **Ice-Forged Greataxe** *(Magic Weapon)*

*Melee Weapon Attack*: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
**Hit**: 1d12 + 5 slashing damage + 1d6 cold damage.

> *(Weapon: Totem-forged greataxe gifted by Red Wizards; enchanted by prolonged exposure to cold magic.)*

---

### **Frost Roar (1/Day)**

Yrga lets out a terrifying, unnatural howl that echoes with cold fury.
All creatures of her choice within **30 ft.** must make a **DC 15 Wisdom saving throw**, or become **frightened** for 1 minute.
Frightened targets may repeat the save at the end of each of their turns.

---

## **Legendary Actions (Optional - Use only for solo boss)**

If you want to run Yrga as a solo boss:

**Yrga can take 2 legendary actions**, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time, and only at the end of another creature’s turn.

* **Cold Slam**: Make a single greataxe attack.
* **Frost Step**: Teleport up to 15 feet to an unoccupied space she can see, leaving behind a slick of ice. Creatures in that space must succeed on a **DC 13 Dex save** or fall prone.
* **Chilling Glare (Costs 2 actions)**: Force one creature Yrga can see within 60 feet to make a **DC 15 Con saving throw** or take **2d6 cold damage** and have disadvantage on the next attack roll or saving throw it makes before the end of its next turn.

## 📖 Combat Notes

* Yrga opens with **Hold Person** or **Command** on high-damage foes.
* Activates **Frozen Heart** early to establish danger zone around her.
* Uses **Reckless Attack** + Rage to tear through front-liners.
* **Armor of Agathys** is often pre-cast if expecting battle.
* If near defeat, uses **Misty Step** or tries to **frighten** enemies to escape.
