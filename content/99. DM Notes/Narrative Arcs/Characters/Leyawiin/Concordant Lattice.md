# 🌀 Part 3: The Concordant Lattice

### Extradimensional Dungeon Crawl (Level 9–10)

---

## 🎭 Premise

The **Concordant Lattice** is an **extradimensional prison-engine**: a fractal maze built of equations and starlight. It was co-designed by Agatha centuries ago to stabilize planar boundaries. Twisted by others, it became a **living labyrinth** that now imprisons Sylvie.

---

## 🌌 **Arc 3 Opening: The Concordant Lattice**

**Narrative Setup (DM Read-Aloud):**
Weeks have passed since Port Celestia. The Astral Codex has lain dormant, its pale light never quite extinguished. Tonight, under the cold stars of Faerûn, the book convulses in Leyawiin’s hands. Pages whip open as if caught in a storm. Threads of starlight coil upward, forming constellations that spin in dizzying rhythm.

The stars begin to blink in measured intervals, then lock into place—twelve points of light snapping into a perfect dodecahedron. The air hums, vibrating like a struck bell.

From deep within the Codex comes a familiar voice—Agatha’s, distant and urgent:
*“Now.”*

The command reverberates in their bones as the book ignites in cold flame. A **Gate** (PHB, p.244) tears open, its rim formed of shifting runes and crystalline light.

---

### **Entry Transition**

* The party steps through the Gate.
* Their senses are pulled sideways, the world unraveling into starlight and silence.
* They reappear aboard the **Tardisian Blue**, its hull gleaming with pale-blue astral fire. Crew members rush about the deck, preparing the ship’s plunge.

At the helm, Agatha’s voice rings out—not from the Codex this time, but in person, commanding from the spelljammer’s bridge:
*“The Lattice opens only once in a lifetime. If you mean to save Sylvie, steel yourselves—this crossing will test more than your strength.”*

The ship banks as the void ahead fractures like glass, revealing a vast crystalline labyrinth suspended in the Astral Sea. The **Concordant Lattice** looms before them—part structure, part living entity, pulsing with cold, mathematical precision.

---

### **Tone & Atmosphere**

* **Shifting Geometry:** Gravity, walls, and orientation alter without warning.
* **Half-Living Crystal:** Surfaces pulse like veins of light; the dungeon seems to breathe.
* **Lawbound Wards:** Sigils and runes rise from the air itself, enforcing order through riddles, traps, and guardians.
* **Semi-Sentient Construct:** The Lattice acknowledges Agatha’s return. It remembers her. And it will test her through the party.

---

### **Primary Goals**

1. **Rescue Sylvie** from her astral bonds.
2. **Survive the extradimensional maze**—traps, wards, adversaries, shifting rooms.
3. **Confront the truth** of Agatha’s role as one of the Lattice’s architects.

---

⚖️ **DM Note:** The entrance should feel momentous—this is not just another dungeon but a thinking construct. Every corridor and encounter feels intentional, designed to test both morality and strength.

---



The Astral Codex acts as the only “map,” but its pages flicker, incomplete. The party must **endure traps, puzzles, and warped guardians** to reach Sylvie, while Agatha confronts the ruin of her past.

---

## 🌌 Environment Traits

* **Gravity Shifts**: Each chamber has its own orientation. When PCs move between nodes, roll 1d6:
  *1–2 = gravity normal; 3–4 = inverted; 5 = no gravity; 6 = shifting every round (DC 14 Acrobatics to adapt).*
* **Lattice Instability**: Psychic resonance permeates the maze. Each short rest, one random PC must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom save or suffer **1 level of exhaustion**.
* **Light**: All areas glow faintly from crystalline walls. *Darkvision is useless here—light is omnipresent but directionless.*

## 📜 Dungeon Flow

The Lattice contains **5 major nodes + Final Chamber**.
Each requires PCs to overcome **trap + adversary + puzzle reward**.

---

### Node 1: Threshold of Equations

**Read-Aloud Description:**
*You step into a hexagonal chamber of perfect symmetry. Glyphs swirl along crystalline walls like living fractals. A bridge of glowing runes stretches ahead, its tiles constantly rearranging.*

* **Trap/Puzzle — Logic Bridge**

  * PCs must deduce safe rune sequence. **Arcana or Investigation DC 15**.
  * Wrong step → tile collapses + **2d8 psychic damage**.

* **Combat — Fractal Sentinels**

  * *Use Helmed Horror (MM p.183)*, reskinned as rune-animated guardians.
  * Immune to psychic instead of fire.

* **Reward**: First **Alignment Rune** for Codex navigation.

---

### Node 2: Hall of Whispering Reflections

**Read-Aloud Description:**
*Mirrored walls stretch into infinity. Yet your reflections move differently—older versions, twisted selves, lives unlived. They begin to whisper… and step free.*

* **Trap — Soul Mirrors**

  * At corridor midpoint, PCs must make **Charisma save DC 16** or be stunned while reflection emerges.

* **Combat — Doppel Reflections**

  * *Use Shadowspawn (FToD p.212)* reflavored as mirror duplicates.
  * Each reflects the PC’s fighting style.

* **Reward**: Sylvie’s faint voice echoes: *“I’m still here. Keep coming.”*

---

### Node 3: Astral Menagerie

**Read-Aloud Description:**
*Crystalline cages line the chamber. Inside float frozen astral beasts, preserved mid-snarl. The glass-like stasis cracks as you approach…*

* **Encounter — Roll 1d3**:

  1. **Astral Dreadnought Fragment** (weakened, CR ~10: 200 HP, half damage resistances, no antimagic cone).
  2. **Star Spawn Seer** (MToF p.236).
  3. **Void Scavenger Swarm** (Star Spawn Mangler, reflavored as void swarm).

* **Hazard**: Area damage risks shattering more cages.

* **Reward**: A fragment of Sylvie’s **Starlit Chain**.

---

### Node 4: Spiral of Unraveling

**Read-Aloud Description:**
*A spiraling shaft of runic chains stretches upward into infinity. Weightless space surrounds you, but gravity shifts violently every few moments.*

* **Trap — Gravity Flux**

  * PCs must make **Athletics or Acrobatics DC 16 each round**.
  * Fail = 4d10 force + psychic damage from impact.

* **Combat — Time-Echo Githyanki Raiders**

  * *Use Githyanki Warrior (MM p.160).*
  * Flavored as aged, broken versions of raiders trapped centuries ago.

* **Reward**: PCs claim a **Lattice Sigil**, keystone rune to breach prison.

---

### Node 5: Chamber of Forgotten Bargains

**Read-Aloud Description:**
*A vast circular hall etched with pact-runes. Whispers swirl—offering each of you an easier path, promises of power, freedom, or escape.*

* **Trap — Temptation Bargains**

  * Each PC makes **Charisma save DC 17**. Fail = charmed + 1 level of exhaustion.

* **Combat — Concordant Warden**

  * Custom monster (CR 12).
  * *Base: Marut (MToF p.213), reflavored as rune-law elemental.*
  * Features: Force Slam (3d10 force), Lawful Judgment (recharge 5–6: 30-ft aura, DC 16 Wis save or restrained).

* **Reward**: Final fragment of Sylvie’s chain.

---

### Final Chamber: Heart of the Lattice

**Read-Aloud Description:**
*A cathedral of fractured crystal. At its center burns a newborn star. Sylvie hangs suspended above it, bound by three radiant chains inscribed with living glyphs. A towering silhouette emerges: the Threshold Keeper.*

* **Objective — Break Chains**

  * Each chain tied to fragments collected earlier.
  * Requires 3 successes (Arcana DC 18, Athletics DC 18, or Codex ritual by Leyawiin).

* **Boss — Threshold Keeper**

  * *Base: Kraken Priest (VGtM p.215).*
  * Legendary Actions: Gravity Shift, Mirror Split, Psychic Wave.
  * Lair Actions: At initiative 20, chains lash at PCs or the floor collapses into void.

* **Resolution**:

  * Sylvie freed, unconscious but alive.
  * Keeper accuses Agatha: *“Your equations still bind the living, Architect.”*

* **Escape**: Collapse begins. PCs must activate Codex portal or be erased.

## 📦 Items & Artifacts

### Starseed Crystal (Canon-Inspired)

*Type*: Spelljamming component.
*Source*: Based on Spelljamming Helm (SJ: AAG p.22).
*Effect*: Required to power the *Tardisian Blue*. Resonates faintly within the Lattice.

---

### Lattice Core Fragments

*Type*: Quest item.
*Effect*: Needed to unlock Sylvie’s chains. Without all three, final chamber cannot be completed.

---

### Astral Codex

*Type*: Wondrous Item (spellbook).
*Effect*: Grants *Plane Shift* (1/week), *Comprehend Languages* (1/day). During Lattice, allows navigation between nodes.

---

### Sylvie

*Friendly NPC (rescued)*

* HP 40 (commoner + protective ward).
* Special: When awake, she grants **1d4 inspiration dice per long rest** (once recovered).

---

### Agatha (Mentor NPC)

*Stat Block*: Use **Archmage (MM p.342)**, but without hostility.

* Role: Guide, lore source.
* During Lattice, she recalls fragments: once per node, grant **advantage on one Arcana check**.

---

## 🎁 Resolution

* **Victory**: Sylvie rescued; Leyawiin gains narrative choice about Agatha’s fate. PCs gain +2 bonus to saves vs. psychic damage (flavored as “Lattice resonance”).
* **Failure**: Keeper absorbs Sylvie. Party escapes, but Sylvie becomes part of the Lattice’s architecture—future quest hook.
