## **Node 1: Threshold of Equations**

### **Read-Aloud Description**

*You step into a hexagonal chamber of perfect symmetry. The air is still, but heavy with an electric tension. Glyphs crawl like living fractals across walls of translucent crystal, folding into themselves with impossible precision. Ahead, a narrow bridge extends into the chamber, formed of glowing rune-tiles that shift and rearrange with each passing second. The floor below is a void of swirling starlight, bottomless and cold.*

---

### **Trap / Puzzle — Logic Bridge**

The bridge is made of **three repeating rows of shifting rune-tiles**. Only one sequence is correct at a time, while the others collapse under weight.

* **Skill Checks**:

  * **Arcana (DC 15):** Recognizes that the bridge responds to mathematical or magical *order*—not brute force.
  * **Investigation (DC 15):** Spots the repeating logic pattern (safe runes align in a sequence across rows).
  * **Insight (DC 15):** Intuits that hesitation or mistrust causes the tiles to destabilize faster.

* **Trap Effect (on failure):**

  * Stepping on a wrong rune → tile collapses. Creature makes **Dex save DC 13** or falls (10 ft before caught by gravity reversal) + **2d8 psychic damage** as the Lattice invades their thoughts with crushing equations.

* **Trap Reset:** The bridge immediately reforms after a mistake, but the sequence shifts.

### **Puzzle Layout Suggestions**

Here are some **approaches for presenting the rune sequence**—you can choose the style that best fits your table:

#### **1. Symbol Categories Puzzle (Classic Logic Grid)**

Create sets of rune symbols—e.g.:

* 🔺 Triangle (stability)
* ● Circle (continuity)
* ✶ Star (transition)
* ▢ Square (foundation)

**Safe Sequence Rule:** The bridge requires choosing runes in a *progression*—for example:

* Triangle → Circle → Star → Square → Repeat.

Players can spot this by watching the shifting runes for a few moments (Investigation check).

---

#### **2. Numerical Rune Pattern (Math Puzzle)**

Runes shimmer with numbers 1–9 in ancient script. Each row forms part of a progression:

* Row 1: 2, 4, 6, ?
* Row 2: 3, 6, 9, ?
* Row 3: 5, 10, 15, ?

**Safe Sequence Rule:** Players must step only on the tiles that *complete the sequence* (evens, multiples of 3, multiples of 5).

---

#### **3. Rhyming Sigils (Word Puzzle)**

Runes spell out syllables in Draconic or Celestial. Safe tiles form a rhyme or incantation.
Example (Celestial script):

* “IL” → “UM” → “NAH” → “EL” (forming a chant: *Ilum’nahel*).

Arcana check reveals the words are part of a hymn to order.

---

#### **4. Color Progression (Simple Visual Cue)**

Runes shift through colors: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, violet.
**Safe Sequence Rule:** Must follow the spectrum forward (ROYGBIV) to cross.
Stepping backward or skipping a color collapses the tile.

* Visual cue: crystalline wall glyphs mirror the correct next shape.

#### 2. **Number Progression**

Runes are numbered in an ancient script (1–9).

* Safe tiles are multiples of 3 (3, 6, 9) → representing “lawful order.”

#### 3. **Color Spectrum**

Tiles glow in colors of the rainbow.

Runes across the bridge reflect glyphs on the walls.

* PCs must step on runes that match the mirrored wall glyphs.
