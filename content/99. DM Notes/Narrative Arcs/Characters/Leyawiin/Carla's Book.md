### *Spellbound and Undressed: Storming the Sorceress’s Tower*

**By: Aelirenya** *(banned in Amn, beloved in Baldur’s Gate) the*

---

**He came for war. She offered… other terms of surrender.**

When **Sir Tharok Ironmane**, a battle-hardened half-orc knight of the Adamant Shield, is sent to bring justice to a rogue spellcaster threatening the borders of Sembia, he expects to storm a tower, seize a staff, and return victorious. What he doesn’t expect is **Velessa Thorne**, a reclusive enchantress whose sorcery is as potent as her sharp tongue and smoldering glances.

Velessa has spent years behind warded doors, studying ancient magic—and burying the scars of a world that punishes female mages who dare claim power. But when a towering knight crashes through her arcane defenses (and into her bath chamber), she finds herself caught between duty, danger… and undeniable desire.

Tharok was sworn never to consort with spellcasters. Velessa vowed never to need a man. But as spells spark and armor clatters to the floor, both will have to choose between the walls they’ve built—and the pleasure they’ve found behind them.
