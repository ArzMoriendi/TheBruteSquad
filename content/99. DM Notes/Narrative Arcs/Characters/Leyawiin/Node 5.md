## Node 5: Chamber of Forgotten Bargains

### **Read-Aloud Description**

*The spiral chains open into a vast circular hall. The floor glows with concentric pact-runes, each inscribed with promises in languages both alien and achingly familiar. The walls breathe with whispers—half in your voices, half in strangers’. Each whisper offers something: an easier path, forgotten power, escape from the burden of the quest.*

*At the far end, an immense figure of crystalline law stirs. Its body is a perfect humanoid shape made from golden fractals, a mask of runes in place of a face. Its voice resonates like a hammer on an anvil:*

**“Bargains made. Bargains broken. All debts return to the Lattice.”**

---

### **Challenge Layers**

#### 1. **Trap — Temptation Bargains**

* When the PCs enter, whispers flood their minds:

  * *“Lay down the Codex. You will be free of pursuit.”*
  * *“Serve me, and I will unmake your enemies.”*
  * *“Turn back, and Sylvie will live—but not with you.”*

* **Mechanics:**

  * Each PC must make a **Charisma saving throw, DC 17**.
  * **Fail:** PC is *Charmed by the Lattice* for 1 minute. During this time:

    * They take **disadvantage** on attacks against the Concordant Warden.
    * They gain **1 level of Exhaustion** as part of their will is siphoned away.
  * PCs may repeat the save at the end of each turn, but each failure whispers another tailored bargain.

* **Counterplay:**

  * An ally can *use their action* to shake or rally a charmed PC with a **Persuasion check DC 15**, granting that PC an immediate save.

---

#### 2. **Combat — The Concordant Warden**

* **Base Stats:** Use *Marut* (MToF p.213). Adjust:

  * Replace **Lightning damage** with **Force damage**.
  * Immune to psychic, resistant to radiant/necrotic.

* **Abilities:**

  * **Force Slam:** Melee Weapon Attack, +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: **3d10 force**.
  * **Lawful Judgment (Recharge 5–6):**

    * The Warden releases a 30-ft fractal aura of binding runes.
    * Each creature in range must make a **Wisdom save DC 16** or become **Restrained by glowing runes** until the end of its next turn.
  * **Immutable Form:** Immune to polymorph or form-altering magic.
  * **Plane Anchor:** Teleportation or planar travel within 60 ft. is suppressed.

* **Tactics:**

  * Warden targets anyone who appears “corrupted” by bargains first.
  * It ignores summons or illusions, attacking only true intruders.
  * If reduced to 0 HP, it doesn’t fall—its body collapses into fractal shards that reassemble in the ceiling. Only destroying its rune-core (see reward) ends it.

---

#### 3. **Victory Condition & Reward**

* After defeating the Warden, the chamber’s whispers fall silent.

* At the center of the rune circle lies the **Final Fragment of Sylvie’s Chain**:

  * A crystalline shard, humming with her voice: *“You’re almost here… just one lock remains.”*
  * Mechanically, this fragment is required to unlock the **Prison Node** where Sylvie is bound.

* PCs who endured the bargains without faltering gain inspiration or DM’s equivalent reward. PCs who fell to temptation but broke free carry faint scars—tattoo-like glyphs that might resurface later as story hooks.

---

### **Narrative Prompts for Roleplay**

* *As each bargain manifests, tailor it to the PC’s backstory or flaws.* For example:

  * To a warlock: *“Forsake your patron. I will be the only power you need.”*
  * To a paladin: *“Lay down your oath. Be free of your burden.”*
  * To a rogue: *“Keep the chain. Use her power for yourself.”*

* During combat, the Warden’s speech is mechanical and inevitable:

  * *“Deviation detected. Correction required.”*
  * *“All debts return. All bargains must be honored.”*

* When the PCs claim the chain fragment, describe the hall’s runes shattering like glass, collapsing into nothingness—only silence remains.

---

✅ **Success:** PCs claim the final chain fragment, giving them the means to breach Sylvie’s prison.
❌ **Failure:** If they fall to the bargains (3+ PCs charmed/exhausted) or the Warden defeats them, they are shackled in runic bonds and ejected from the Lattice back into Faerûn, scarred and marked as oath-breakers.
