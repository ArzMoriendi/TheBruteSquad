# 🪐 SPELLJAMMER ARC PREP – PART 2

### **Port of Whispers**

## ⭐️ Act I – Call Across the Veil

**Timeline:** Weeks or months after the events of Chult (pace this with your ToA travel/jungle crawl).

### Narrative Prompt

> One humid night in Chult, the **Astral Codex** begins to thrum with pale-blue light. The runes shift, beating like a second heartbeat in Leyawiin’s pack. A voice pierces her mind — calm, hurried, familiar:  
> _“Port Celestia breached. Tardisian Blue has arrived. Come now—before the gate closes.”_

- **Mechanics:** A **Sending** spell (PHB p. 274) cast by **Agatha** or **Nymar**.
    
- The Codex vibrates like a tuning fork, resonating with astral magic.
    

**Transport Options:**

- **Gate** (PHB p. 244): An ally (Agatha, Nymar, or another astral patron) opens a shimmering portal of runes, pulling the party directly onto the Tardisian Blue.
    
- **Dream of the Blue Veil** (TCoE p. 106): If you prefer a dreamlike, slower transition, the party is drawn into a shared dream where the ship sails through their visions before they awaken aboard.
- 
## ⭐️ Act II – Boarding the Tardisian Blue

**Boxed Text:**

> The world fractures into ribbons of light. Cold astral winds tear across your face as the jungles of Chult vanish. You stand upon the creaking deck of the **Tardisian Blue**. Her fractured sails glow faintly, shimmering with runes that shift and collapse into themselves. The corridors echo, empty, haunted by whispers of the dead.  
> Ahead, suspended in the Astral Sea, looms a port of impossible scale — **Port Celestia**, a city carved into the ribs of a dead god. Its docks flare with astral fire, already breached by strange attackers.

- PCs arrive **mid-flight**.
    
- **Nymar’s ghost** or **Skive (autognome)** works frantically to stabilize the helm.
    
- Immediate RP opportunity to reconnect with allies (Nymar, Shrike, Agatha’s projection).
    
- Urgency: The Blue is on final approach to Port Celestia.
---

## ⭐️ Act III – Location: Port Celestia (aka Port of Whispers)

- **Built in the corpse of a dead god.** Its skull is a cathedral of trade; its ribcage holds the docks.
    
- **Commerce hub of the Astral Sea**, neutral ground enforced by mercenary guilds.
    
- **Notable factions**:
    
    - **Githyanki Raiders** (outlaws here, but present in secret).
        
    - **Celestial Traders** (angelic bureaucrats & neutral outsiders).
        
    - **Whisper Brokers** (run black markets of secrets and memories).
        
        **Revelation:** Agatha demonstrates how the **Codex + Lattice Core Fragment** reveal Sylvie’s prison inside the **Concordant Lattice**.
---

## ⭐️ Key NPCs

### 1. **Agatha** (Neutral Good Simulacrum of a lost archmage)

- **Role**: Quest giver, ally.
    
- **Appearance**: Woman of ice and starlight, flickering slightly as though unreal.
    
- **Personality**: Wry, compassionate, with undertones of sadness.
    
- **Goal**: To unravel the Codex before her essence fades.
    
- **Stat Block**: Use **Archmage** (MM p. 342) with **HP halved** and vulnerability to fire (fragile illusion).
    

---

### 2. **Shrike** (returning ally)

- If the PCs saved him in Part 1, he helps navigate Port Celestia.
    
- Knows the underbelly of the city and the Whisper Brokers.
    

---

### 3. **Whisper Broker – Keleth Zarn**

- **Race/Class**: Tiefling, Neutral Evil.
    
- **Role**: Black market dealer in **Last Whispers** (final words, bottled).
    
- **Stat Block**: **Mage** (MM p. 347), add trait _Whisper Trade_: knows a secret about every PC.
    
- **Hook**: Can provide info on Sylvie’s captors — for a price.
    

---

## ⭐️ Adversaries

### **Void Scavenger Pack (Astral Dockside Thugs)**

- Use **Star Spawn Mangler** (MToF p. 235) but reflavored as astral scavenger-thieves.
    
- Ambush the party while they leave the Whisper Market, attempting to steal the Codex.
    

---

## ⭐️ Quest Items

### **Lattice Core Fragment**

- **Description Block:**
    
    > A shard of crystalline geometry, ever-shifting like a kaleidoscope. When held, faint glowing threads extend from it, pointing into infinite horizons.
    
- **Function**: A physical piece of the **Lattice**. Used to track Sylvie’s location.
    
- **Mechanics**: Once per day, can cast _Locate Creature_ (PHB p. 256), targeting Sylvie.
    

---

## ⭐️ Encounters

1. **Dockside Roleplay:**
    
    - PCs meet Shrike/Agatha.
        
    - Discover Sylvie is alive, held within the Lattice.
        
2. **Whisper Market:**
    
    - Black market stalls sell bottled whispers, stolen dreams, mementos of the dead.
        
    - Narrative Hook: Tie to Pale Flame’s request (Last Whispers, Soul-Touched Relics).
        
3. **Ambush in the Alleys:**
    
    - **2 Star Spawn Manglers** attempt theft.
        
    - If defeated, they dissolve into void ichor, whispering Sylvie’s name.
        
4. **Lattice Revelation:**
    
    - Agatha uses the Codex + Lattice Core Fragment.
        
    - Reveals Sylvie’s prison: the extradimensional labyrinth known as **The Concordant Lattice**.
