## 🪞 **Node 2: Hall of Whispering Reflections**

### **Read-Aloud Description**

*The corridor ahead stretches impossibly long, its floor and walls made of perfect mirror-crystal. Reflections ripple outward into infinity, repeating your every movement. But as you draw closer, you notice the reflections no longer move in time with you—some lagging, others racing ahead. A few do not mirror you at all, but instead show older versions of yourselves, twisted and warped, lives you never lived. The reflections begin to whisper—hissing doubts, half-familiar voices—and one by one, they step free of the glass.*

---

### **Trap — Soul Mirrors**

At the midpoint of the corridor, a line of crystalline mirrors hums with psychic resonance.

* **Trigger:** Any PC walking within 10 ft of the midpoint must make a **Charisma save DC 16**.
* **Effect (Fail):**

  * PC is **stunned until end of next turn**.
  * During the stun, their reflection tears itself out of the mirror to join combat.
* **Effect (Success):**

  * PC resists the pull but hears whispers of regret (*“You should have chosen differently…”*). They take **5 psychic damage** as the Lattice tests their will.

> ⚖️ *DM Note:* The trap should feel personal—use reflection whispers that touch on each PC’s backstory or past decisions.

---

### **Combat — Doppel Reflections**

The reflections take shape as crystalline mockeries.

* **Stat Block:** *Use **Shadowspawn (FToD p.212)**.*

  * Reflavor as **Mirror Doppel Reflections**.
  * Replace cold damage with **psychic damage**.
  * Appearance: jagged crystalline silhouettes, glowing cracks where their “faces” should be.
* **Tactics:**

  * Reflections mimic the fighting style of the PC they split from.
  * They target their “original” first, whispering *“I am the better you.”*
  * If “their” PC drops unconscious, the reflection dissolves in shards of mirror.

---

### **Environment Prompts**

* **Mirrored Walls:** Spells like *Shatter* or *Dispel Magic* can crack or destabilize the mirror walls. Breaking a mirror deals **2d8 psychic damage** to all nearby creatures (PCs and reflections).
* **Reflections Multiply:** If combat drags on, roll at initiative count 20—another reflection claws free unless mirrors are disrupted.
* **Illusionary Terrain:** The mirrors can create false passages. An **Investigation DC 14** reveals which corridors are real.

---

### **Victory Conditions**

* **Defeat the Reflections:** Destroying them causes the mirrored corridor to stabilize.
* **Clever Bypass:** Covering mirrors, breaking them strategically, or using magic like *Protection from Evil and Good* can prevent additional spawns.
* **Trap Disarm:** A **Dispel Magic** or **Arcana DC 17** check suppresses the Soul Mirrors entirely.

---

### **Reward**

Once the last reflection falls, the mirror corridor grows still. The reflections dissolve into shimmering motes that vanish into the crystal walls.

From nowhere and everywhere at once, Sylvie’s faint voice echoes, tired but resolute:
*“I’m still here. Keep coming.”*

Each PC feels a rush of reassurance. They may each remove **1 level of exhaustion** or regain **1 expended hit die** as Sylvie’s presence steadies them.
