# 🪐 SPELLJAMMER ARC PREP – PART 1

## ⭐️ Act I: Unlocking the Codex in Chult

### Narrative Prompt

> **Read Aloud:**  
> As night falls, Leyawiin feels the Codex pulse with a strange warmth, its runes writhing like ink alive on parchment. A voice cuts through the humid air, calm and resonant: _“You wish to see, child of the script? Then let me lend you my sight.”_

### New NPC – **Kethra the Runebinder** (Chult, neutral ally)

- **Race/Class**: Yuan-ti Pureblood, Neutral Good (exiled from local tribes).
    
- **Background**: Obsessed with ancient glyphs and planar lore, she wanders Chult offering her gift of temporary runesight.
    
- **Narrative Function**: She grants Leyawiin the effect of **Eyes of the Runekeeper** (via _Arcane Eye + custom ritual_).
    

**Mechanical Effect**:

- Ritual: 1 hour
    
- Grants **Eyes of the Runekeeper** (UA/SCAG, “You can read all writing”) for 24 hours.
    
- Source: Homebrew ritual based on _Comprehend Languages_ (PHB) + _Arcane Eye_ (PHB).
    

**Stat Block**: Use **Mage** (MM p. 347) but swap _Fireball_ for _Glyph of Warding_ and add advantage on History/Arcana checks involving runes.

## ⭐️ Act II: The Tardisian Blue

### Arrival (triggered when Leyawiin reads the Codex)

**Effect**: _Plane Shift_ (PHB p. 266)

- Description: The book explodes with starlight. The party feels their bodies unravel and reweave in silver threads before suddenly standing on the deck of a colossal, ruined ship drifting in a starry void.
    

---

### Location: The Tardisian Blue

**Description Block:**

> A Spelljammer ship drifts silently in a sea of silver mist and distant constellations. Its once-proud sails hang shredded, glowing faintly with arcane energy. Crystals along its hull are cracked and guttering. Strange debris — fragments of statues, bone, and steel — floats in orbit around it.

**Features:**

- **Gravity Plane**: Standard Spelljammer rules (SJ:AiS p. 7).
    
- **Hazards**: Psychic echoes (Wisdom save DC 13 or take 2d6 psychic once per long rest).
    
- **Exploration**: Crumbling corridors, dead crew (skeletal remains), fragments of battle reports etched in fading ink.
    

---

## ⭐️ Key NPCs

### 1. **Nymar Vey** – Ship’s Navigator (Neutral Good Elf Ghost)

- **Description**: A translucent elf spirit, speaking in fragments of memory. Loyal, melancholy.
    
- **Stat Block**: **Ghost** (MM p. 147) but cannot possess. Can use _Guidance_ at will.
    

### 2. **Skive** – Autognome Repairman (Chaotic Good)

- **Description**: Rusted, patched, missing one eye lens. Comic relief. Constantly repairing nothing.
    
- **Stat Block**: Use **Autognome (SJ:AiS, Boo’s Astral Menagerie p. 19)** but friendly.
    
- **Quirk**: Repeats: “Fix the helm, fix the helm, fix the helm—oh wait, I can’t find the crystal.”
    

### 3. **Shrike** – Half-Gith Mercenary Survivor (Neutral, battle-worn)

- **Description**: Suspicious of the party. Only cares about escaping.
    
- **Stat Block**: **Veteran** (MM p. 350) but with a silvered longsword.
    
- **Hook**: Reveals the ship was boarded by **Void Scavengers** and **Githyanki Raiders**.

## ⭐️ Adversaries

### 1. **Void Scavengers**

- **Lore**: Aberrant scavengers feeding on astral remains.
    
- **Stat Block**: Use **Star Spawn Grue** (MToF p. 236) with added trait: _Astral Drift_ (can teleport 20 ft. as a bonus action).
    

### 2. **Githyanki Boarding Party**

- **Description**: A trio of stranded raiders, still haunting the ship.
    
- **Stat Block**: **Githyanki Warrior** (MM p. 160).
    
- **Twist**: They are “echoes” (half-real). When reduced to 0 HP, they vanish in starlight.

## ⭐️ Quest Item

### **Starseed Crystal**

- **Description Block:**
    
    > A pulsating shard of crystallized starlight, cool to the touch, humming softly like a heartbeat. When placed into the Spelljammer helm, it glows and floods the ship with radiant energy.
    
- **Effect**: Required to activate the helm.
    
- **Mechanics**: Functions as a **Spelljamming Helm Fuel Source**. Without it, ship remains adrift.
    
- **Source**: Homebrew; modeled after **Spelljamming Helm** (SJ:AiS p. 22).
    

#### Option A: **The party has what’s needed already**

* A party spellcaster (Leyawiin?) unknowingly carries a **resonant crystal** (e.g., something gifted by Agatha long ago, now revealed as a backup *starseed*).
* Or it’s part of an old magic item Agatha infused with redundancy for emergencies (ex: **a spell focus**, **a brooch**, **an earring**, etc.).

🧠 **Lore Reference**:
Arcane helms are powered by spellcasters, but exotic ships can require rare foci. This is a flavorful extension of *Spelljammer: Astral Adventurer’s Guide*, p.22.

#### Option B: **The starseed is among the wreckage**

* During the battle with **void scavengers**, the party finds the needed component in a **wrecked pirate vessel** or inside a **stolen cargo crate** aboard the ship.
* May require **Arcana check (DC 16)** to identify, and **Repair check** (using *mending*, *fabricate*, or tools)
* Found embedded in the chest of a dead Githyanki Echo. Requires Strength **(Athletics DC 15)** to pry free.

#### Option C: **The party crafts or improvises it**

* With access to the ship’s damaged components and *fabricate* (PHB p.239), they can forge a temporary **starseed conduit**.
* Or Leyawiin can pour **a fragment of her magical essence** into the helm temporarily (gaining 1 level of exhaustion as cost)

## ⭐️ Encounter Flow

1. **Boarding the Blue**
    
    - Exploration, meeting Nymar, Skive, Shrike.
        
    - Learn about the missing Starseed Crystal.
        
2. **Scavenger Ambush**
    
    - While searching cargo decks, attacked by 2d4 **Void Scavengers**.
        
3. **Crystal Recovery**
    
    - See options above
        
4. **Helm Room Finale**
    
    - As the Starseed is installed, 3 **Githyanki Warriors** manifest, trying to prevent escape.
        
    - Nymar aids with spectral _Guidance_.
        

---

## ⭐️ Resolution

- With the helm powered, the **Tardisian Blue** launches.
    
- Nymar whispers: _“The Codex is not only a map. It is a key.”_
    
- A familiar voice from The Codex whispers: 
	>"You’ll need to wait until the stars converge again. When the time comes, we’ll find you—even in the deepest jungle or the blackest crypt. Be ready." 
- Ship deposits party back to Chult using a **Material Anchor Beacon** (homebrew teleport beacon).
