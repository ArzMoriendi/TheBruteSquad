# ⚡ Mechanic: Releasing Eremon from the Statue

### **1️⃣ Core Concept**

Eremon’s statue prison is a **divine-magic seal**, keyed to **Zwargie’s moral and spiritual state** as well as **external arcane events**. He can be released either **actively** (through Zwargie’s actions) or **passively** (through unintended circumstances).

---

### **2️⃣ Activation Conditions**

#### **Option A: Moral/Spiritual Trigger (Primary)**

Eremon’s prison weakens as Zwargie accumulates **Whispered Zeal points** (from obeying or yielding to whispers, committing morally dubious acts).

| Whispered Zeal Total | Effect on Prison                                                                                                               |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 0–2                  | Statue is stable; only minor vibrations of light                                                                               |
| 3–5                  | Statue begins to glow faintly; small radiant fractures appear (foreshadowing)                                                  |
| 6+                   | Statue cracks; Eremon can make a Wisdom saving throw to attempt **semi-conscious communication** (spells, whispers, influence) |
| 9+                   | Full release possible if triggered by a major act (see below)                                                                  |

**Mechanics:**

* **Each major morally dubious act (killing innocents, children, fleeing trial, resisting trial combat)** → +1 Whispered Zeal
* **Roleplay compliance with Eremon whispers** → +1 Whispered Zeal
* **Resisting whispers or showing mercy** → -1 Whispered Zeal

Once **Whispered Zeal ≥ 9**, Eremon is ready to break free mechanically, but needs a **trigger event**.

---

#### **Option B: Trigger Event (Secondary / Narrative)**

The actual release occurs when one of the following happens:

1. **Arcane Energy Event:**

   * Zwargie or an ally uses **high-level necromancy, planar magic, or divine smite equivalent** near the statue (Level 5+ spells: *Dispel Magic*, *Flame Strike*, *Hallow*, *Raise Dead*, etc.)
   * Each such cast requires a **DC 16 Arcana save (STAT: Zwargie + allies)** to avoid shattering the seal.
   * Success: Statue cracks but Eremon remains partially bound; failure: Eremon breaks free fully.

2. **Divine-Soul Alignment:**

   * Zwargie’s Oath of Redemption feature triggers a moral choice that aligns **with Eremon’s definition of “divine will”**. Examples: executing a villain to prevent greater evil.
   * DM rolls **DC 18 Wisdom save**; failure triggers partial or full release.

3. **Forced Physical Damage:**

   * A sufficiently strong strike can break the statue (optional).
   * AC 22, HP 120. DMG types that affect magical constructs: *force, radiant, magical weapons*.
   * Destroying the statue releases Eremon instantly.

---

### **3️⃣ Partial Release / Manifestation**

Before full freedom, the following can occur:

| Effect                       | Narrative / Mechanics                                                                                 |
| ---------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Eremon whispers increase     | +2 Whispered Zeal gain per morally dubious action                                                     |
| Minor spectral manifestation | Eremon can cast 1–2 CR 5–6 spells through whispers (*Guiding Bolt*, *Flame Strike*, *Blinding Smite*) |
| Semi-corporeal influence     | Attempts to possess or control Zwargie if Whispered Zeal ≥ 7 (Wisdom save DC 17 to resist)            |

### **4️⃣ Full Release Mechanics**

* Requires **Whispered Zeal ≥ 9** OR **statue destroyed OR triggered by spell/arcane act**
* Once released:

  * Eremon enters **Final Judgment Form immediately**
  * Gains **full stat block** (CR 17–18 Planetar)
  * Whispers now **permanent, cannot be resisted**, become direct telepathy during combat

**Optional DM Flavor:**

* Statue fragments explode with radiant energy, dealing 6d6 radiant damage in a 20-ft radius (DC 18 Dex save for half)
* Any undead or evil-aligned creatures within 60 ft automatically frightened for 1 round
