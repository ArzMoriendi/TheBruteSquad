### **Andraeus, Bladesinger of the Emerald Grove**

*Medium humanoid (wood elf), neutral good*

**Armor Class** 17 (mage armor) or 22 with Bladesong
**Hit Points** 91 (14d6 + 42)
**Speed** 35 ft.

---

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| :-----: | :-----: | :-----: | :-----: | :-----: | :-----: |
| 10 (+0) | 18 (+4) | 16 (+3) | 20 (+5) | 14 (+2) | 11 (+0) |

---

**Saving Throws** Int +9, Wis +6
**Skills** Arcana +9, Acrobatics +8, History +9, Perception +6, Stealth +8
**Damage Resistances** psychic (from Mind Blank), force (frequently uses Absorb Elements)
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 16
**Languages** Common, Elvish, Sylvan
**Challenge** 10 (5,900 XP)
**Proficiency Bonus** +4

---

### **Special Traits**

**Fey Ancestry.** Andraeus has advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put her to sleep.

**Bladesong (2/Short Rest).** As a bonus action, Andraeus can activate her Bladesong for 1 minute, granting:

* +5 bonus to AC
* +10 ft. speed
* Advantage on Acrobatics checks
* +5 bonus to Concentration checks

**Arcane Deflection (Reaction, 1/turn).** When hit or forced to make a saving throw, Andraeus can use a reaction to gain +2 AC or +4 to the saving throw until the start of her next turn.

**Song of Victory.** While Bladesong is active, she adds her Intelligence modifier (+5) to her melee weapon damage rolls.

---

### **Actions**

**Elven Thinblade (Rapier of Speed).** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
*Hit:* 1d8 + 4 (DEX) + 5 (INT) piercing damage. Can make an additional attack with the weapon as a bonus action.

**Cantrips (at will):**
*Mage Hand, Fire Bolt, Minor Illusion, Booming Blade, Prestidigitation*

**Spellcasting.** Andraeus is a 14th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). She has the following wizard spells prepared:

* **1st (4 slots):** *Mage Armor, Shield, Absorb Elements, Detect Magic*
* **2nd (3 slots):** *Mirror Image, Misty Step, Blur*
* **3rd (3 slots):** *Counterspell, Haste, Dispel Magic*
* **4th (3 slots):** *Greater Invisibility, Dimension Door*
* **5th (2 slots):** *Wall of Force, Telekinesis*
* **6th (1 slot):** *Globe of Invulnerability*
* **7th (1 slot):** *Forcecage*

---

### **Reactions**

**Defensive Flourish.** If targeted by an attack, she can cast *Shield* or use Arcane Deflection.

---

### **Legendary or Recurring NPC Flavor (Optional)**

**Tactics.** Andraeus avoids direct confrontation unless necessary. In combat, she opens with *Haste*, activates *Bladesong*, and uses *Booming Blade* in combination with her thinblade. She controls the battlefield with *Wall of Force* or *Forcecage*, and escapes using *Dimension Door* or *Greater Invisibility*.

**Roleplay Note.** A mysterious and graceful arcane duelist, Andraeus defends ancient elven secrets. She may assist or oppose the party depending on how much they respect the old ways of magic and nature.
