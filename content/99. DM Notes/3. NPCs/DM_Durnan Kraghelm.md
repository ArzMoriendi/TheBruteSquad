## 🧍‍♂️ **Captain Durnan Kraghelm**

**Race**: Shield Dwarf  
**Class**: Veteran Fighter  
**Role**: Senior Captain in the Axe of Mirabar (the city’s elite guards)  
**Personality**: Stern, suspicious, duty-bound, deeply loyal to Mirabar’s dwarves and mines.  
**Accent**: Deep, gravelly voice with a clipped dwarven dialect.

---

## 🎭 **General Roleplay Tone**

- **Public Voice**: Gruff, no-nonsense, borderline rude when uncertain.
    
- **Private Voice (if trust earned)**: Deeply respectful of duty and dwarven honor, but weary of politics and threats to Mirabar.
    
- **Secret Pressure**: The Council has warned him to **tighten inspections** — reports of Red Wizard interference and missing dwarven relics have the Axe on edge.
    

---

## 🎙️ **Roleplay Prompts by Situation**

---

### 🛑 **Arrival at the Gate — Initial Challenge**

> _Suspicious and forceful tone, dwarves behind the walls watching carefully._

- **"Halt right there! Caravan or not, we’ve got strict orders. No one gets through without the Captain's eyes on 'em."**
    
- **"That Goliath of yours — where’s he from? Looks like he's walked out of a bad mountain memory..."**
    
- **"Furbolg, stay where ye are. Ain’t seen your kind ‘round here since the orc trade wars."**
    
- **"State your names, affiliations, and purpose in Mirabar. Slowly. One wrong word, and you're staying outside these gates."**
    

---

### 🎯 **Interrogating Veldar — Suspicion Toward Goliaths**

> _Directly challenges the Goliath. Hostile but not irrational._

- **"You. Goliath. What tribe? We’ve had Ironclaw raiders spotted two days ago — if you’re with 'em, we’ll know soon enough."**
    
- **"Your kind tore through Hammerfall Run twenty winters ago. This better not be a reunion tour."**
    
- **"Your arms are tattooed with old blood. You can say you’re civil now, but the axe remembers."**
    

---

### 🗣️ **Persuasion Under Way — Players Try to Talk Their Way In**

> _Tone shifts from suspicion to scrutiny._

- **"Hrmph. Fine words, but good liars sound the same as good men. Why should I trust you won’t torch a trade hall or slit a jeweler’s throat?"**
    
- **"You’ve got a priest and a wizard. That tells me you’re either adventurers... or assassins. Let’s hope it's the first."**
    
- **"You want in? Convince me you’re not a threat to Mirabar. Not just talk. Make me believe it."**
    

---

### ⚖️ **If They Pass the Check — Begrudging Respect**

> _Grim, still cautious, but allowing passage._

- **"You’ve got stones, I’ll give you that. Fine. You can pass — but you’ll be watched. Closely."**
    
- **"The Hall of Sparkling Stones will want to speak with you. Don’t try to leave the city till they’re done."**
    
- **"Escort them to the underhall. And keep that Goliath at the front, where I can see his back."**
    

---

### 🔒 **If They Fail the Check — Arrest Veldar or All**

> _Cold, decisive, disarming tone._

- **"Enough talk. Veldar, was it? You’re coming with us. You can explain your past to the Council’s inquisitors."**
    
- **"We warned you. The city’s had enough blood from Goliath blades. Take him."**
    
- **"Resist, and I’ll bury you in paperwork _and_ a holding cell."**
    

---

### ⚔️ **If the Players Attack or Resist**

> _Commands barked with deadly authority._

- **"Axe of Mirabar! Shields up! Take them down nonlethal — the Council wants them breathing!"**
    
- **"Sound the forge-horns! We've got trouble at the North Gate!"**
    
- **"You fools chose the wrong city for a brawl. We built this place with war and stone!"**
    

---

### 🧩 **If They Surrender or Cooperate After Arrest**

> _Curt and controlled tone, leaves the door open for redemption._

- **"Smart choice. Could mean you’re not as mad as you look."**
    
- **"Mirabar doesn’t kill without trial. You’ll get your word. Best choose your truths carefully."**
    
- **"You might still walk free. Depends on what the Council hears. Or finds."**
    

---

### 🤝 **If Trust is Earned Later (e.g., during the quest)**

> _Softer tone, reveals deeper sense of duty._

- **"I’ve buried kin at Hammerfall Run. If you find what happened out there... you’ve got my thanks. And more."**
    
- **"Mirabar’s got its flaws. But it protects its own. If you’re among us, I’ll fight for you."**
    
- **"Tharna Ironclaw... if she’s back, it’s not just your past at risk. It’s ours too."**
