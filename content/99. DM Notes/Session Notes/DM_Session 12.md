### Scene Setter
 
Our adventurers find themselves on the outer side of the northwest bridge exiting Mirabar, having just secured Veldar's release and being given an opportunity to clear his name.  The party is tasked with investigating Hammerfall Run and why they've stopped communicating.

As they prepare to depart, Daeric comes running out and asks Shadow Scale's help to track down a missing vendor (a grocer).  He agrees, handing off the party materials to Sarhice. 

The party finds and follows the sparse trail up the mountain side, noting it gets colder and more sparse as it gets higher altitude. 

They come upon a wrecked carriage, off the trail, behind some bushes.  Exploring the carriage, Veldar notes strange glyphs, red cloth, and marks of an attack.  Leyawiin investigate and determine the glyphs are Thayan necromantic glyphs.  Bronn casts detect magic and senses two sources of necromantic magic further up the hill side. 

Veldar moves the some materials and discovers a Glyph of Warding.  The party recognizes it as a trap trigger and hastily makes a plan to trigger the trap. They move off the path and hide. 

Veldar triggers the trap, causing cold damage.  Leyawiin casts minor illusion, putting three party members next to Veldar. The explosion causes two Goliaths to come running.  They confront Veldar, and quickly determine he is not an ally.  As they prepare to attack, the party takes surprise attack actions. 

The battle moves quickly, with Veldar taking a majority of the damage.  After a short while, a dwarven skeleton appears.  Using Turn Undead, Bronn wards off the undead creatures, causing them to flee.  

As combat continues, Leyawiin fells one Goliath with Magic Missile, Sarhice slices through dwarven skeleton, Bronn casts Guiding Bolt and destroys a human skeleton.  In a decisive action, Zwarg slices through the toe of the last Goliath, killing him. 

Veldar retrieves the skeleton, and the party loots the bodies, finding a total of 2 gold, 2 silver, 10 copper, and a pair of "Fitting Gloves" which are given to Sarhice.  Bronn casts Gentle Repose on the Goliaths. 

Party - 2 gold, 2 Silver, 5 Copper  
Bronn - 5 copper  
Sarhice - Fitting Gloves

The party moves off the path, makes camp, and settles in for a long rest. (Northwest).
