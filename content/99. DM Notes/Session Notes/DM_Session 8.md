### Scene Setter
Our adventurers find themselves in the treasure room of the Flying Drunks, having dispatched Kragnoroth, his lieutenant, and attendants. Taking measures to ensure safety, they lock themselves in the room and take a long rest.

During the rest, two of our adventurers have visions, one in secret. Shadow Scale finds himself in a vision where he sees three flames: one neutral colored (gray, brown, beige, ruddy red), one colorful (red, orange, blue, green), and one metallic (silver, gold, platinum, bronze). Approaching each and hearing voices, he learns he must make a choice. He learns they are the dragonic deities [[Bahamut]], [[Tiamat]], and [[Asgaroth]]. He chooses Asgaroth. He wakes to a drake incarnation. (He is now a Drakewarden Ranger).

Zwargie attempts to attack the drake but Shadow Scale and Leyawiin stop him.

The party awakens, and attempts to leave, finding the door locked. Kragnoroth's key does not work. They go through the keys and find Vanessa's works. They divvy up the recently found goods.

They leave and exit the caves through hidden shaft, finding themselves in a grove of trees at early afternoon. They begin making their way to [[Erentir]], the new party staying together for a time. Leyawiin notices a waterfall and the group inspects it, determining this was where the Drunks did initiations, and where Ned's son died. Zwargie makes a double waterfall.

They camp and agree to do shifts, two people to a shift. As Zwargie and Leyawiin are guarding, Zwargie hears a noise. Before Leyawiin can wake anyone, Zwargie hears a voice that says "Go. help." And he runs off toward the sound. Leyawiin follows, angry that he just ran off.

They come upon an elf and halfling fighting off a ghoul and two skeletons. They help dispatch the creatures and meet [[Aribyn Deepcreek]] (halfling paladin) and [[Andraeus Naeyarus]] (elf wizard). They explain this was the last of a nest of undead and they were nearly overwhelmed. Aribyn recognizes Zwargie as a member of the Order of Enlightened Orcs, a sub order to the Order of Aster. She hands him an edict from the Justiciar and encourages him to take up the call. He has taken the Oath of Redemption.

In thanks for helping, Andraeus Naeyarus teaches Leyawiin Bladesinging. They return to their camp, everything undisturbed.

Bronn and Sarhice's shift was uneventful.

During Shadow Scale and Veldar's shift, Shadow Scale had an urgent call of nature. While gone, a large beast came up stalking Veldar. Veldar grappled with the beast. Back and forth, the creature and Veldar were matched strength. Finally the beast ran away leaving Veldar empowered with the Path of the Beast.

The party awoke and made their way into town. They stopped by the Tavern and found [[Erentir|Ned]] passed out drunk at the bar. They took him home. They obtained smelling salts from an apothecary, went back and woke him up. He promised them payment if they came back the next day.

They obtained a shirt and pants from a clothier for Veldar.

The party went to the Magistrate's office to seek out their reward. They learned Franz was hung for lying and usurping power. The Magistrate ([[Erentir|Shosven Peacesworn]], human, in fine, we'll kept robes but looking distinctively careworn in face.) offered a small amount of gold for reward (50, upped to 100). He told them they may be able to go to Luskan for more. He asks who they are, and they decide to call themselves "The Brute Squad." He writes a letter that covers meals and board at *The Farmer's Daughter* while they are in Erentir as additional repayment and sends it to [[Erentir|Triss]].

They spend the night, Leyawiin with Valerie the serving girl, Sarhice and Bronn in a room. Shadow Scale and Veldar in the stables, and Zwargie out by the tree. Zwargie hears a voice stating "You have done well. Continue forth and vanquish evil."

Next day, they returned to Ned's, passing wheelbarrows and carts along the way of what appears to be armorer/blacksmith items. They learned that Ned is selling his stuff. After intimidating the buyers out of the equipment, they return it to Ned. They learn he doesn't have the 500 GP, believing they wouldn't be successful. Now he intends to sell everything to make good in his debt. After a negotiation, the Brute Squad accepts 50 Gold, a promise of a Double Edged axe and potentially other Armor from Ned as repayment.

They return to town and encounter a large carriage and display of wares and a very loud man exclaiming the goods. The party sees ceremonial masks akin to those from Africa. They see pottery goods and jewelry similar to Meso-American style. They see beautiful feathers and large teeth and bones (as if from lizards).

The barker is a large man, well-built upper body with bright red hair, cut short, and a large moustache. He's wearing explorer's clothes, khaki and green. He has a broad smile to match his broad shoulders.

The man notices them and asks if they are interested. He shows a drawer of precious jewels, emeralds, lapis lazuli, and more. He offers them a deal to map the area, finding treasure, and offers to sponsor the adventure. He says he has offices in [[Neverwinter]] and [[Waterdeep]]. The party agrees on Neverwinter and shakes the man's hand. They ask his name: [[Boniface Abernathy]].

The party stops there.

Loot: 150 gp.
