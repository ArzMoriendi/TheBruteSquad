### Scene Setter
After a long rest, our party wakes in the evening hours. Jethro the wolf has ran off.   Loreli and Artiran are deep in discussion.  Loreli tells the party that this isn't what she expected and her old bones are a bit sore.  She's off to find opportunities elsewhere and Artiran is going with her.  B'Imbo croaks that he wants to go with the old woman.  The three of them move off on their own adventure. 

Leyawiin, Shadow Scale, and Zwargie discuss next steps.  Shadow Scale suggests going into town to find more adventurers and the other two agree.  They begin traveling towards Erentir.  As they follow the path, they hear a noise coming towards them.  After a while, a very battered elf appears stumbling towards them.  He tells how he was ambushed and his party killed by a gang of thugs. They ask him his name and if he can recall anything about the gang. Sarhice recalls the logo of the Flying Drunks and the name [[Kragnoroth the Drunk]]. The party decide to continue their adventure and seek revenge. 

They descend the shaft and continue forward until they hear the sounds of fighting. They come upon two orcs and two bandits training.  Zwargie attempts to stealth to them, failing miserably.  The orcs spot him and Leyawiin and attack.  Leyawiin makes quick work of one, Shadow Scale kills one of the bandits, and Sarhice and Zwargie helps hinder another.  Sarhice attempts to connect to his natural magic and fails, but connects with a dark, more primordial magic.  The group spots a dwarf chained to the wall who helps the party with a bit of magic, allowing Sarhice to deliver the killing blow using his new, magical power.

They free the dwarf, who introduces himself as Bronn. He is a cleric from water deep, traveling through the area. Bronn was taken in after cheating while gambling with the flying drinks. He quickly equips himself and tells them about another prisoner a little further up. 

The group moves forward to find a hulking figure chained up, wearing only a "banana hammock" underwear.  They talk to him and learn he is Veldar, a goliath barbarian from the Spine of the World.  He was captured after "the bad man" told him to steal wine and he got caught and got in trouble.  Veldar is simple man who speaks in small words and few syllables. 

The party invites him to join them, and Veldar flexes, breaking his chains.  He fetches his maul and pants, and the party continues forward (after Bronn drinks a handful of wine).

The party moves forward, managing to sneak past a Giant Spider lair, coming upon all cavern and a door.  As they move forward, the door opens and a light appears.  Shadow Scale and Bronn identify an Orc and four humans.  Veldar, Zwargie, and Bronn manage to hide, while the adversaries engage Sarhice, Shadow Scale, and Leyawiin.  Veldar smashes in a guard head, Zwargie crushes a chest, and Shadow Scale and Sarhice dispatch the other two guards.  Bronn, using inflict wounds, dispatches the orc leader, grabbing him by the balls and destroying him with magic.  He tips the testicles out of the scrotum and carefully pockets the fleshy pouch.  He uses sacred flame to end the last remaining bandit. 

Loot: 1 potion of healing, 24 CP. 

The party pauses before moving onward.
