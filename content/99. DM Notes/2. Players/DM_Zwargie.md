Solve mystery of trinket
- Identify the creature inside
- Fight it
Redeem himself from causing the deaths of his companion 


Several days ago, the Order of Enlightened Orcs, a subsect of Order of the Aster conducted a raid on a mage's tower in the foothills of the Craggs.  In the process, Zwarg touched an obelisk/statuette that spoke to him, "free me".  Touching the obelisk, triggered a magical trap that decimated the other paladins, and the mage (a Red Wizard of Thay) disappearing in the aftermath.  The voice in the statue (a Planetar) protected Zwarg.  He woke up miles away, with his memory lost.
