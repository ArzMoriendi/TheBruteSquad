## Backstory

Bronn grew up outside [[Waterdeep]] with his parents until they both passed. He lived on the streets of Waterdeep for a little while before studying at the temple to become a cleric/priest of [[Kelemvor]]. He chose the path of [[Kelemvor]] due to his unresolved grief losing his parents and found it was a good way to cope with that loss in service to other going through the same loss of various loved ones. [[Kelemvor]] teaches followers to guide those in mourning and those in their final moments as well as guide the dead to their final resting place. Through the learnings as a priest they were also taught to have great disdain for the necromantic arts and all forms of undead. (This is why he consecrates corpses after fights.) Due to his fondness of drink and dice he was sent on a tour of penance of sorts, but under no supervision, he's come to just take his talents on the road making money where he can performing last rights or funerary services, and occasionally disposing of the odd undead here and there.
