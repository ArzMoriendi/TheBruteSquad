## Backstory

Leyawiin, a fledgling sorceress, found herself in a whirlwind of wonder and uncertainty. Her beloved mentor, the eccentric wizard Agatha, had left her a curious inheritance: a magical bookstore filled with enchanted volumes and whispering shelves. Among these treasures, Leyawiin stumbled upon a strange, ancient text, its pages radiating an otherworldly power.
Intrigued and a bit apprehensive, she knew she couldn't ignore this enigmatic book. Agatha, unfortunately, had passed away before completing Leyawiin's magical education, leaving her with a yearning for knowledge and a responsibility to unravel the mysteries of the ancient text.
But running Leyawiin's Literary Labyrinth, with its quirky customers and demanding spells, required full attention. Leyawiin, with a heavy heart, entrusted the shop to her capable sister, Sylvie. It was time to embark on a journey, following the faint trail left by Agatha, seeking out her former acquaintances and fellow wizards. Perhaps they held the key to understanding the origins of the book and could even teach her the advanced spells Agatha never had the chance to share.
With determination in her heart and the ancient text tucked safely away, Leyawiin stepped out into the wider world, ready to face whatever challenges lay ahead. It was a journey of discovery, a quest for knowledge, and a chance to honor the memory of her beloved mentor. And who knows, perhaps along the way, she would not only uncover the secrets of the ancient text but also find her own path to becoming a powerful sorceress in her own right.
