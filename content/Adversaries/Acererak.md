## **Acererak** — *The Devourer, The Eternal, Architect of Death*

### 🕯️ Symbol & Appearance

* **Symbol / Holy Icon (Cult)**: The “Sign of the Devourer” — a stylized green demonic face with a gaping maw, found etched on walls within the Tomb of Horrors and among his cult’s devotees .
* **Physical Manifestation**: In the *Tomb of Annihilation*, Acererak appears as a demilich — a floating skull with jeweled ruby eye-sockets and diamond teeth, draped in rotting robes. He wields the **Staff of the Forgotten One**, bears a **talisman of the sphere**, and controls a **sphere of annihilation** during his final confrontation .

---

### 📜 History & Important Lore

* **Origins**: Acererak is a cambion, born of a human mother and the balor demon **Tarnhem**, whom he later imprisoned. He studied under **Vecna**, eventually surpassing his mentor and constructing death-trapping tombs to lure adventurers and harvest their souls for his phylactery .
* **Tomb of Horrors**: Originally built in Oerth in service to Orcus, it became his signature death-trap dungeon. Many fell unwary into its deadly machinations, and it formed the basis of his fearsome reputation .
* **Omu & Tomb of Annihilation**: In Chult (\~1480s DR), Acererak slew the Nine Trickster Gods of Omu, enslaved the city's people, and forced them to build the **Tomb of the Nine Gods**. Within it, he created the **Soulmonger**, an artifact designed to birth an **atropal** and trap souls, unleashing the **Death Curse** across Toril .

---

### ⚔️ Adversaries & Allies

* **Adversaries**:

  * The **adventurers** who enter his tomb to destroy the Soulmonger and end the Death Curse.
  * **Acererak’s phylactery defenders**, including those adventurers or groups like the Skullbreakers .
* **Allies & Minions**:

  * Enslaved undead and constructs, including winterwights and arch‑wraiths.
  * His cult followers span liches, necromancers, and the **Black Academy** founded near the Tomb of Horrors. Many undead and skilled necromancers pledge service to him, storing phylacteries in his tomb network .

---

### 🧭 Responsibilities & Motivations

* **Motive**: Acererak is not interested in worship or divinity; instead, he delights in constructing elaborate tombs filled with traps to lure powerful adventurers, kill them in dramatic ways, and trap their souls. He once quipped: *“Why be a god when I can be a creator of gods?”* .
* **The Soulmonger Project**: Finding a drifting atropal in the Negative Plane, Acererak built the Soulmonger to harness massive influxes of souls—particularly those resurrected—to feed and accelerate the atro pal toward godhood. He viewed that delay of death and prevention of resurrection as a power play over life and undeath itself .

---

### 🧪 Tomb Final Encounter (Tomb of Annihilation highlights)

* **Stat Block**: Acererak is a CR 23 arch‑lich with INT 27, CHA 20, legendary resistances, regeneration, and true sight. He wields the Staff of the Forgotten One, a talisman of the sphere, and commands a sphere of annihilation in battle .
* **Battle Mechanics**: When PCs destroy the Soulmonger, Acererak attacks alongside four tentacles guarding a lava pit. Trickster-god–possessed PCs gain temporary HP and bonus psychic damage when hitting him. If reduced below 100 HP, Acererak casts **Teleport** and flees, vowing vengeance .

---


### 🧠 Additional Context

* **Cult of the Devourer**: A necromantic cult centered around Acererak venerates instability and seeks to guide adventurers into his tombs as offerings. The Black Academy in Skull City grew around remnants of his worship .
* **Post-Acererak Legacy**: Although the Soulmonger can be destroyed, Acererak may not be truly gone; his phylactery remains hidden somewhere, and his lichic essence roams the multiverse. Rumor surrounds attempts to rebuild or usurp his dark designs—though few succeed .
