
The Sword Coast refers to Faerûn’s western shoreline along the Sea of Swords, from Icewind Dale and the [[Spine of the World]] in the north down to Cormyr, Amn, and parts of Tethyr in the south. 

[[Waterdeep]], [[Neverwinter]], [[Mirabar]], and [[Luskan]] are major or notable cities in the area known as the Sword Coast.
