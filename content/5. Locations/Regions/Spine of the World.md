The Spine of the World was a mountain range that stretched across the northwest region of Faerûn. The Spine of the World mountains shielded the civilizations of the North from the freezing expanse of the Uttermost North.

Home to frost giants, yeti, and other creatures, many Goliath tribes found a way to live in the barren peaks. 

[[Veldar]] and his Frostfang tribe came from the Spine.
