## **Kelemvor** – _Lord of the Dead, Judge of the Damned_

### 🛐 Portfolio & Domains

- **Portfolio**: The Dead (souls, proper transition to the afterlife) ([realmshelps.net](https://www.realmshelps.net/deities/Kelemvor?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Kelemvor - Deities of Faerûn - Human Pantheon"), [nwnarelith.com](https://nwnarelith.com/ea/index.php/Deities%3A_Kelemvor?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Deities: Kelemvor - Encyclopedia Arelithica"))
    
- **Domains**: _Death_, _Grave_ (as used in D&D 5e) ([Forgotten Realms](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Kelemvor?utm_source=chatgpt.com "forgottenrealms.fandom.c..."))
    
- (Older editions list additional domains such as Fate, Law, Protection, Repose, Travel; but 5e highlights only Death and Grave.)
    

### ⚖️ Symbol & Appearance

- **Symbol**: A skeletal arm holding balanced scales — emblematic of judgment and order in death ([nwnarelith.com](https://nwnarelith.com/ea/index.php/Deities%3A_Kelemvor?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Deities: Kelemvor - Encyclopedia Arelithica"))
    
- **Divine Avatar**: Typically depicted as a lawfully neutral figure in chainmail or gray robes, stoic and impartial ([Forgotten Realms](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Kelemvor?utm_source=chatgpt.com "forgottenrealms.fandom.c..."))
    

### 📖 History & Lore

- **Mortal Life**: Born Kelemvor Lyonsbane, a human mercenary cursed by lycanthropy. He adventured alongside Midnight (later Mystra) and Cyric. During the Time of Troubles, he fell in battle, his soul siphoned by Mask (in disguise as the sentient sword Godsbane) ([World Anvil](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/ahmarath/a/kelemvor-article?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Kelemvor Character in Ahmarath | World Anvil")).
    
- **Ascension**: After a decade in exile and a revolt within the City of the Dead, Kelemvor overthrew Cyric and was acclaimed Lord of the Dead around 1368 DR — succeeding Myrkul and later Cyric in the office ([World Anvil](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/ahmarath/a/kelemvor-article?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Kelemvor Character in Ahmarath | World Anvil")).
    

### 🧭 Responsibilities & Doctrine

- Guides souls impartially and ensures fair judgment.
    
- Teaches that **death is natural and dignified**, not to be feared — only the wicked (the Faithless or False) need dread what comes after life ([nwnarelith.com](https://nwnarelith.com/ea/index.php/Deities%3A_Kelemvor?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Deities: Kelemvor - Encyclopedia Arelithica")).
    
- Views **all undead as abominations**, ordering their systematic destruction regardless of origin or intent ([nwnarelith.com](https://nwnarelith.com/ea/index.php/Deities%3A_Kelemvor?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Deities: Kelemvor - Encyclopedia Arelithica")).
    

### 🏛️ Clergy, Organization & Rituals

- **Worshipers**: The dying, their families, gravediggers, morticians, undead hunters, mourners ([nwnarelith.com](https://nwnarelith.com/ea/index.php/Deities%3A_Kelemvor?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Deities: Kelemvor - Encyclopedia Arelithica")).
    
- **Vestments**: Smoky gray robes and cowled cloaks. Rank indicated by badge color (iron for novices, silver for priests, gold for senior clergy) ([World Anvil](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/tales-of-faerun-autumnfyr/a/kelemvor-article?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Kelemvor Character in Tales of Faerun | World Anvil")).
    
- **Daily Rituals**:
    
    - _Remembrance_: Morning graveside prayers at cemeteries.
        
    - _Daeum_: Evening communal service of hymns and readings in a temple setting.
        
    - Clerics pray for spellcasting at nightfall, symbolizing souls transitioning inward ([World Anvil](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/not-forgotten-realms-palant/a/church-of-kelemvor-article?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Church of Kelemvor Organization in Not Forgotten Realms | World Anvil")).
        
- **Holy Days**:
    
    - _Shieldmeet_ (every four years, midsummer) – ceremonies recounting heroic ancestors (“Deeds of the Dead”).
        
    - _Feast of the Moon_ (Nightal, early winter) – powerful clergy may perform true resurrection for past heroes ([World Anvil](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/not-forgotten-realms-palant/a/church-of-kelemvor-article?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Church of Kelemvor Organization in Not Forgotten Realms | World Anvil")).
        
- **Favored Weapon**: A bastard sword called **Fatal Touch** ([realmshelps.net](https://www.realmshelps.net/deities/Kelemvor?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Kelemvor - Deities of Faerûn - Human Pantheon")).
    

### ⚔️ Adversaries & Allies

- **Greatest foes**:
    
    - _Cyric_: Murdered Kelemvor in life and abused the portfolio of death. Constant schemer against Kelemvor and his tenants.
        
    - _Talona_: Harbinger of diseases and premature death, disrupting the natural transition.
        
    - _Velsharoon_: Necromantic deity who animates undead in defiance of Kelemvor’s doctrine ([World Anvil](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/forgotten-realms-djverne12/a/kelemvor-person?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Kelemvor Character in Forgotten Realms | World Anvil"), [World Anvil](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/tales-of-faerun-autumnfyr/a/kelemvor-article?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Kelemvor Character in Tales of Faerun | World Anvil")).
        
- **Allies**:
    
    - _Mystra_ (Midnight): Former lover and divine ally.
        
    - _Jergal_: Original scribe of the dead who now serves Kelemvor by recording fates.
        
    - _Lathander_: Shares opposition to undead and supports life’s renewal ([World Anvil](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/tales-of-faerun-autumnfyr/a/kelemvor-article?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Kelemvor Character in Tales of Faerun | World Anvil"), [World Anvil](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/ahmarath/a/kelemvor-article?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Kelemvor Character in Ahmarath | World Anvil")).
        

### 🧠 Personality & Legacy

- Kelemvor is **lawful neutral**, earnest, forthright—though lacking in subtlety. He solves issues through direct and sometimes harsh action, tempered with kindness and dignity ([petesfr.wikidot.com](https://petesfr.wikidot.com/printer--friendly//the-church-of-kelemvor?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Pete's Forgotten Realms: The Church Of Kelemvor")).
    
- His message: _"Death is part of life, not punishment. Accept it with honor. Respect the dead, preserve their memory."_ ([World Anvil](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/forgotten-realms-djverne12/a/kelemvor-person?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Kelemvor Character in Forgotten Realms | World Anvil"), [nwnarelith.com](https://nwnarelith.com/ea/index.php/Deities%3A_Kelemvor?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Deities: Kelemvor - Encyclopedia Arelithica"))
