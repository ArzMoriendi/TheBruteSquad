Tiamat -  *The Dragon Queen, Queen of Chromatic Dragons*
Evil dragons and reptiles, conquest, greed, vengeance, law (as control), and tyranny. She is the ultimate patron of chromatic dragons across Faerûn and beyond
Alignment: Chaotic Evil
Symbol: A stylized five‑headed dragon, each head the color of a chromatic dragon (white, black, green, blue, red)
Directly opposed to [[Bahamut]]
