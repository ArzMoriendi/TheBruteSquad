Bahamut - *The Platinum Dragon, King of the Good Dragons, Lord of the North Wind*
Good and metallic dragons; justice; protection of the weak; wisdom; noble leadership. In Faerûn, he is closely allied with deities of law and compassion such as Torm and Moradin
Alignment - Lawful Good
Symbol: A platinum dragon’s head in profile
Directly opposed to [[Tiamat]]
