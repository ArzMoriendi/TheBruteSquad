Here is an official 5th‑Edition (5e)–era profile of **Bahamut**, drawn from *Wizards of the Coast* 5e source material (*Player’s Handbook*, *Fizban’s Treasury of Dragons*, *Hoard of the Dragon Queen*, etc.) and lore summarized on the *Forgotten Realms Wiki*. All details reflect his current Faerûnian/draconic status.

---

## **Bahamut** — *The Platinum Dragon, King of the Good Dragons, Lord of the North Wind*

### 🛐 Portfolio, Domains & Responsibilities

* **Portfolio**: Good and metallic dragons; justice; protection of the weak; wisdom; noble leadership. In Faerûn, he is closely allied with deities of law and compassion such as Torm and Moradin .
* **5e Divine Domains**: *Life*, *War* (as specified in the Player’s Handbook and affirmed in *Fizban’s Treasury of Dragons*) .
* **Responsibilities**: Upholds enlightened justice—justice tempered with mercy. Champions protection of innocents, oppose tyranny, and guide his followers to do deeds of good rather than showy worship .

---

### 🎨 Symbols & Appearance

* **Symbol**: A platinum dragon’s head in profile (often subtle and stylized) .
* **Appearance**: Depicted as a majestic platinum dragon, often over 100 feet long with shimmering silver-white scales and bright blue or indigo cat‑like eyes, embodying serenity and authority .
* **Alternate forms**: May manifest as a kindly old man or humble persona (e.g. traveling as a halfling or canary), especially in *Hoard of the Dragon Queen* and other Realms appearances .

---

### 📜 Lore, History & Key Myths

* **Origins**: Son or divine heir of Io (Asgorath), created as the embodiment of mercy, honor, and protection among dragons. In some traditions, Lendys and Tamara are his siblings rather than offspring .
* **Great Conflict**: Arch-rival of Tiamat—their clash nearly sundered reality. Their face‑off above Gehenna generated the Battle of Khalas Chasm, after which Tiamat was bound to Avernus by Asmodeus. Bahamut continues to oppose her influence and followers vigorously .
* **Faerûnian Presence**: His worship increased during the Spellplague era when he joined the Triad of Good. He played a direct role in the Bloodstone Lands via Gareth Dragonsbane and the Tree-Gem, and has followers active in Damara and Tymanther .

---

### ⚔️ Adversaries & Allies

* **Adversaries**:

  * *Tiamat*—Queen of Chromatic Dragons and symbol of draconic evil. Her cultists and spawn are primary foes.
  * *Evil dragons*—especially chromatic types that oppose metallic-aligned ideals.
* **Allies**:

  * *Good-aligned deities* like Torm, Moradin, Ilmater, Yondalla, and Heironeous.
  * *Metallic dragons*—especially gold, silver, brass types—hold him in particular reverence; many gold wyrms serve as his exarchs or lieutenants .

---

### 🧭 Worship, Clergy & Practices

* **Worshipers**:

  * Metallic dragons (gold, silver, brass) revere him as their divine king.
  * Many humanoids also follow him—particularly dragonborn knights and paladins.
  * In Faerûn, human, elven, halfling, and others answer his call (e.g. the *Rite of Rebirth*, where a devotee becomes dragonborn) .
* **Clergy & Orders**:

  * Clerics are exclusively good-aligned (LG or NG), strive toward justice and mercy without zealotry.
  * Followers consider their good deeds as prayers, preferring action to chanting.
  * Some orders land the title Platinum Knights or Vassals of Bahamut, sworn to combat evil dragons and uphold moral order .
* **Temples & Shrines**:

  * Rare; often simple shrines within dragon lairs marked by his symbol.
  * Humanoid temples are minimalist, with clean architecture and emphasis on meditation and hospitality to the needy—“worship with deeds.”
* **Rituals**:

  * *Rite of Rebirth*: Initiates are baptized at dawn and renamed as dragonborn, gaining partial platinum dragon heritage as champions of his cause .
