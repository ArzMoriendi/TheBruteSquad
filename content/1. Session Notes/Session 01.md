### Scene Setter

Everyone is awake and having just feasted on eggs, people leave.  They learn the name of the bartender ([[Triss Hardin]]) and talk to the other patron in the tavern ([[Erentir|Daldir]]) about Kragnoroth.  Daldir gestures westward when asked where he can be found.  Keep the hill in sight. The group leaves _The Farmer's Daughter_ and look around town. They note there's a group standing outside the magistrate's office.  After cutting in line, they enter a pillared and marbled hallway where a harried man stands there with quill and parchment.  The group finds out this is Franz, the magistrate's clerk.  Upon questioning, [[Erentir|Franz]]
tells the group that [[Kragnoroth the Drunk]] is a wanted criminal and there's a 1500gp bounty.  Will pay for magistrate's seal (stolen) and "proof".  Zwargie here's a voice that says "Go.... Kill..."

They head west out of town and travel a little over six miles to the edge of the forest where the road turns northward.  They enter forest and walk a couple of miles. The trees get taller, darker.  They encounter 5 kobold; two run away.  They kill the 3.  While they loot kobolds, the other 2 come back with an owl bear.   They battle and kill the owl bear and kobold; B'imbo lands the fatal blow with magic missile, flaying the owl bear.  

Zwargie came back with 1 HP.    ~~Artiran was knocked unconscious and healed.~~  Artiran was raging so 1/2 damage.
