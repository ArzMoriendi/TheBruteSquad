### Scene Setter
After defeating the orc and humans, the party moves through the door.  Making their way carefully down the hallway, they encounter four human bandits outside of a large door. Through a combined effort between all of the partygoers, they dispatch the four bandits.  The party attempts to pick the lock of the door and fail to open it.  They turn their sights toward the tunnel path and follow it around.  They again encounter a door.  Zwargie moves forward to bang on the door, triggering a dart trap.  He is injured, taking some poison damage. Leyawiin and Bronn offering healing in preparation, and Bronn casts Bless on Zwargie.  The party knocks on the door and a voice encourages them to "enter when they are ready"

The open the door encountering two human guards and the leader, [[Kragnoroth the Drunk]], seated at a table with his lieutenant.  He attempts to parlay, but Zwargie barges in and stands on the table.  Shadow Scale dispatches one of the guards, to which Kragnoroth shrugs and makes an offhand remark.  Zwargie intimidates Kragnoroth, taking [[Kragnoroth the Drunk|Vanessa]]'s head from Shadow Scale and throwing it before Kragnoroth, but Kragnoroth is undeterred.  He offers the party an opportunity to join his crew and do business on his behalf.  There's treasure in a new area and he wants the party to represent him and his interests.  Shadow Scale encourages the party to take time to consider the offer, with Sarhice and Leyawiin being the most vocal to say no.  They return and turn him down and begin battling him. 

Zwargie engages Kragnoroth, grappling him.  Shadow Scale engages the lieutenant.  Veldar and Leyawiin begin attacking the other guard.  After a time, the guard falls.  Kragnoroth breaks free of his grapple, to be engaged by Bronn and Sarhice.  After multiple efforts, Sarhice lands the killing blow.  After, the lieutenant is quickly dispatched.  They loot the bodies and the space, finding 200 gold, a key and 20 more gold and the magistrates seal.  Shadow Scale beheads Kragnoroth. 

The party uses the key to open the locked door. They inspect for traps, seeing the same style of entry as that of Kragnoroth's door.  They use one of the dead bandits to trigger the trap. They find assortment of gems worth 40 GP, a key, and an additional 12 SP, plus enchanted loot. 

Loot: Treasure room key,  Head of Kragnoroth, 40 GP worth of Gems, and 220 GP, 12 SP. Magistrate's Seal. 

- spellbook, scroll of healing word, gauntlets of ogre power, moonlit two handed sword, mystery key, cast off plate armor.

The party stops on a long rest.
