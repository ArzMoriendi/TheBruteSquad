### Scene Setter
 
Our adventure resumes with the Brute Squad on the Long Road.

The party had accepted a ride from a tall, friendly stranger named [[Daeric Elmlight]].  They learned later that he is a firbolg, a giant-kin with bovine features.  

He asked the party more information about themselves, where they were from, how they came together, and information about where they were headed. 

They talked through their journey up the long road, camping overnight.

They talked about [[Kragnoroth the Drunk|Kragnoroth]] and how they were headed to [[Luskan]].  Daeric had heard of Luskan and told them a little of what he knew about the pirate lords. 

He told them about his background (came from a firbolg druid conclave), settled in [[Longsaddle]] and runs a farm and apiary where he peddles grains, wines, and mead.  Specifically Elmlight Gold. He has a wife, Marta, and three kids: Tirna, Orlem, and Bree.

Bronn, Zwargie, and Veldar shared mead with Daeric, getting nice and toasty. 

Leyawiin slept in one of the tents, Daeric slept in a makeshift cover on his wagon, while most of the rest of the party slept on the ground or a bedroll.  Zwargie borrowed Leyawiin's book, "Spellbound and Undressed: Storming the Sorceress’s Tower", reading a few pages.

They finished their trip to Mirabar, chatting with Daeric along the way. 

On approach, they were waylaid by guards from Mirabar. They halted the company, pointing weapons at Veldar. The Brute Squad successfully talked their way through and learned Mirabar was suffering from recurrent Goliath attacks.  As a simple creature, Veldar wasn't able to defend himself.  He was taken into custody and the party was told where to find him (the Hall of Sparkling Stones)

The party attempted to enter Mirabar and were again halted due to lacking papers.  Daeric graciously vouched for them and they were let in.  The Squad helped Daeric unload and set up his wares and he led them to the Hall of Sparkly Stones.

The party met Geldryn Axebinder, Justiciar of the Marchion of Mirabar.  He explained the situation and told the party Veldar would be tried and executed the next day if found guilty.  The party argued on behalf of Veldar. Successfully persuaded, Geldryn had Veldar brought up and listened to his case.  The Justiciar and the party agreed to a task to show they were not associated with the Goliaths conducting raids and that they were no harm.  They would investigate the goings on and would travel to Hammerfall Run and find out why the camp went dark.  Rumors and messages told of shadows, large raiders, and some kind of dark sorcery

The party had until nightfall to depart.  They went back to the market where Zwargie purchased a two handed greataxe in trade for chainmail armor from Ghorza gra‑Bagol at the Greystone Smithy.  They made their way back to the Hall, secured papers of entry for the party, and took Zwargie (hidden in a carriage) to the outermost ring/bridge of Mirabar (Northwest).
