### Scene Setter
 
Our heroes find themselves in Erentir, presented with an opportunity to work for [[Boniface Abernathy]], a gregarious business man and seeming adventurer. He is traveling around, showing off various artifacts from a recently "discovered" region called [[Chult]]. He offers our heroes a deal which they agree to take and he tells them to meet him either at his offices in [[Neverwinter]] or [[Waterdeep]]. After a brief conversation, and at Leyawiin's urging, they agree to Neverwinter, being closer.  Bronn was hoping for Waterdeep. 

They then go shopping, stopping by the textile merchant to pick up Veldar's shirt. Not knowing the best way to go, they head to the magistrate's office and look at the map in the main foyer.  After discussing, they determine the safest tout would be the main road north to [[Mirabar]], then west to [[Luskan]], and finally south to Neverwinter. 

They then head to the general store and purchase bedrolls and tents, haggling the manager down to 8gp and 1sp for 3 bedrolls and 3 tents for the party.

Their last stop is at the stables to see if they can rent or purchase a mule or horse.  After discussing the costs, they determine that they will trek and carry their gear.  

They depart the town and head north. After a day of travel, they make camp on the side of the road. As they prepare to bed down, they talk about their past and experiences.  We learn that Sarhice is from Longsaddle and Veldar is from the Spine of the World.  They agree to take shifts for watch and bed down.  During Leyawiin's shift, she spots a shape and wake's Sarhice.  They determine it's a bear and use a spell to convince the bear to depart.  

No other shifts reported any issues. However, Leyawiin notices several items missing when she woke as did Bronn.  A quick inventory showed the following items missing:
Leyawiin: spellbook, lamp, tinderbox, parchment, dagger
Veldar: banana hammock
Bronn: lamp, wineskin, tinderbox, dice
Zwargie: tinderbox, lamp, hunting trap, waterskin
Shadow Scale: hunting trap, waterskin,

and all of the party gold (381 pcs.)

A search of the area revealed three-toed prints leading in towards the Crags. Shadow Scale identified them as Kobolds.  

The party followed the trail towards the Crags for the better part of a day, when a tall tower loomed in the horizon. Zwargie broke away and went to investigate, a thorough search of the grounds revealed a mound radiating dark magic, which Bronn was unable to consecrate/dispel.  The party went in and went all the way to the top of the tower and to the basements. The tower showed signs of battle and was scorched on the inside, with nothing being salvageable. In the bottom-most basement, a plinth stood.  Activated by a memory, Zwargie pulled out a spherical statue that fit perfectly on the plinth in the spaces there.  When questioned, he knew nothing.  The voice in his head asked "why have you brought me back here? You rescued me from this space." They made their way outside.  

End inside the tower.
