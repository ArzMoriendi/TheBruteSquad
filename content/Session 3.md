### Scene Setter
Finding themselves at the entrance of the cave, the party takes stock of where they are. Loreli realizes she's carrying too much and chooses to divest herself of her mace (goes to Artiran).  B'Imbo soaks for 1 hour (required for Grung).  Leyawiin and B'Imbo use Arcane Recovery to get back a spell slot. The party moves into the caves.

They travel in until the come onto a party of 4 bandits playing cards.  They attack quickly and dispatch the bandits, taking minimal damage.  Loreli pissed herself as a bonus action.
Loreli takes two Aces hidden up the sleeve of one bandit.   XP: 15 each (25 per bandit)

22 cp

The move further into the tunnel, coming onto a lavishly decorated room.  Silk pillows, couch, table, comfortable setting. Two female bandits and one Yuan-Ti (Vanessa). Kallí attempts to throw B'Imbo into the Yuan-Ti, failing the strength role and B'Imbo failing the acrobatics role.  They dispatch the bandits quickly and take a moment to fight the Yuan-Ti.  She drops hints about her love and never getting out.  They kill her as she tries to flee.  Artiran beheads her using Fey Step.  (Loreli uses Toll the Dead on bandit).  Shadow Scale takes the head.  XP: 36  (25 per bandit; 200 for Yuant-ti Pureblood).  Potion of Healing - Shadow Scale.  Key - Shadow Scale.   Investigation reveals "V+K" carved into the table. 

17sp

Going deeper into the caves, the party hears music.  They role for stealth (nat 20 - DC 25);  Come into a large hall with two large tables.  4 bandits sit at one table playing music, and a goat-like creature sings and dances with a silver chalice.  Zwargie enters (thinking stealth).  Too bright, the Satyr welcomes him and offers him a beer when he asks for beans.  The party enters, the music stops and Satyr challenges them asking "Where's Vanessa".  When Shadow Scale says "Who's Vanessa?" the bandits attack.  Zwargie and 1 bandit rolled 1s (miss a round).  Party takes minimal damage and dispatches the satyr first, followed by the bandits.  Satyr is killed by Loreli/Toll the Dead.  Necrotizes to dust.  Loreli kills final bandit/Toll the Dead.  XP: 29 (25 per bandit; 100 for Satyr).  Potion of Healing - Shadow Scale.  Scroll of Animal Friendship Potion of Animal Friendship- Kallí. 

18 cp

Total XP each 80

Total Treasure 40cp 17sp 2x Potion of Healing, 1 x Scroll of Animal Friendship, 1 x key.

Misc: 2 x Aces, 1 x Yuan-Ti Pureblood head, 1x Satyr Skull

Party ends on a Short Rest.
