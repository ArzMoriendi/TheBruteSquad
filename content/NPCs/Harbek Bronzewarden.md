## **Traits**

### **Dwarven Resilience**

Advantage on saving throws against poison; resistance to poison damage.

---

### **Sculpt Spells (Evocation)**

When Harbek casts an evocation spell that affects other creatures he can see, he can choose a number of them equal to 1 + the spell’s level. Chosen creatures automatically succeed on their saving throws and take no damage if they would normally take half damage.

---

### **Potent Cantrip**

Creatures that succeed on saving throws against his cantrips still take half damage.

---

### **Empowered Evocation**

Add +5 (INT modifier) to one damage roll of any wizard evocation spell.

---

### **Overchannel (1/Long Rest without penalty)**

When casting a 1st–5th level spell that deals damage, Harbek can deal maximum damage.  
Subsequent uses before resting deal necrotic damage to him (standard Overchannel scaling).
