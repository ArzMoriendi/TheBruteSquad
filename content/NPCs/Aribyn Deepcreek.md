Backstory: A Radiant Life

Aribyn Deepcreek grew up in the sun-dappled meadows of Deepcreek Hollow, a village bursting with laughter, garden festivals, and old songs. Born into a loving family of bakers and musicians, Aribyn was always drawn to the morning light and the stories of heroes who protected the innocent.

Her devotion to Lathander, god of rebirth and renewal, blossomed early. As a child, she would gather the younger halflings at dawn to lead “sunrise parades,” where they danced and sang until the dew dried on the clover. Her faith was never a burden—it was joy itself.

At 16, she joined the Order of the Aster, a knightly sect devoted to spreading light, hope, and healing. Unlike some who were drawn by tragedy or vengeance, Aribyn joined out of pure love for life and the conviction that the world could be better if enough people chose to protect it. She believes every sunrise is a second chance—for the world, and for herself.

But her peaceful mission took a focused turn when she learned of the Wizards of Thay—necromancers who twisted life into mockery, enslaving the dead and living alike. Their crimes weren’t just evil—they were insults to Lathander’s light. Aribyn vowed that she would become a radiant shield against their darkness, bringing the dawn to Thay itself if needed.

Now, at level 10, Aribyn is a beacon of cheer and courage. She uplifts her allies, humbles her foes, and strides into danger with a shining smile and a sword blessed by the morning sun.

Lay on Hands (50 HP/day)

As an action, Aribyn can heal a creature she touches, restoring up to 50 hit points per long rest. She can also spend 5 points to cure one disease or neutralize a poison.

Channel Divinity (1/rest): Peerless Athlete

For 10 minutes, Aribyn adds +4 to Strength (Athletics) and Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks, can jump further and higher, and ignores difficult terrain when climbing.

Aura of Protection (10 ft.)

Allies (including herself) within 10 feet add +4 (CHA mod) to all saving throws.

Aura of Alacrity (10 ft.)

Allies that start their turn within 10 feet gain +10 ft. movement until the end of the turn.

Glorious Defense (Reaction, 3/day)

When an ally within 10 feet is hit by an attack, Aribyn can use her reaction to impose disadvantage on the attack roll. If it misses, she can grant another creature within range a +4 bonus to AC until the end of their next turn.
