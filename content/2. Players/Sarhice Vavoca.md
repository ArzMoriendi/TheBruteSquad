## Backstory

Sarhice was previously a druid with an armed escort party (Heither/fighter, Shelia/paladin, and Parmenus/rogue). A pacifist by nature he always tried to come to peaceful resolutions whenever possible. 

The entire group was killed by bandits including the merchant they were escorting that day. Sarhice found himself alone days later. Finding everything of value taken, the only things remaining besides the corpses of his friends and client was a silver coin halfway embedded in the mud next to his head and Heither's broken sword. The coin being slightly larger than standard currency coins and with never before seen symbols on it, he took it with him out of curiosity.. 

Since awakening, Sarhice no longer has access to his druidic magic and doesn't know why. Filled with an unknown source of vengeance, Sarhice only wants to avenge his comrades and find peace.
