### Backstory
Sarhice was previously a druid with an armed escort party (Heither/fighter, Shelia/paladin, and Parmenus/rogue). A pacifist by nature he always tried to come to peaceful resolutions whenever possible. 

The entire group was killed by bandits including the merchant we were escorting that day. Sarhice found himself resurrected as a hollow one undead days later. Finding everything of value taken, the only things remaining besides the corpses of his friends and client was a silver coin halfway embedded in the mud next to his head and Heither's broken sword. The coin being slightly larger than standard currency coins and with never before seen symbols on it, he took it with him out of curiosity. Upon realizing he's undead, he's filled with shamed that he's been taken out of the circle of life. 

Since awakening, Sarhice no longer has access to his druidic magic and doesn't know why. Filled with an unknown source of vengeance, Sarhice only wants to avenge his comrades and return to death.
