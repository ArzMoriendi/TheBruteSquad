### Backstory
Artiran’s origins trace back to an ancient Eladrin warrior clan, revered for their battle prowess and wisdom. Born under the omen of a blood moon, Artiran was destined for greatness, though her path would be marred by tragedy. A catastrophic betrayal from within her clan left her village in ruins, and Artiran, the sole survivor, vowed vengeance.

Struggling to harness the seething rage within her, Artiran found herself wandering the vast expanses of the Material Plane. Fate led her to encounter Loreli, an elderly human woman with a storied past as a healer, through an adventuring party they were both part of. Loreli took her under her wing thinking she was her granddaughter. Though they are of no blood relation, Artiran dare not correct her in this. 

Loreli’s knowledge of ancient rituals and healing arts helped Artiran channel her fury into formidable strength, making her an indomitable barbarian. With Loreli’s wisdom and Artiran’s unmatched combat skills, they form an inseparable bond, their combined efforts making them a formidable force against the shadowy enemies they face.

Their journey is one of revenge, redemption, and uncovering hidden truths. As they traverse perilous landscapes and confront countless evils, Artiran and Loreli prove that the strength of their bond and resilience can overcome any obstacle. Though Loreli is blissfully unaware of the revenge blank seeks.
