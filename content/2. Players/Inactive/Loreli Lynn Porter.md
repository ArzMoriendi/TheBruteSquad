### Backstory
Loreli Lynn Porter has lived a long and eventful life. Born in the small village of Goldenfields, she was the daughter of the local healer. From a young age, Loreli showed a natural aptitude for healing and a deep compassion for others. She spent her youth learning the ways of herbalism and prayer, eventually becoming a devoted cleric of the god of protection, Helm.

Loreli married a kind-hearted farmer named Walt and together they raised a large family. Her children and grandchildren were the light of her life, and she was known throughout the village as the loving matriarch who always had a warm hug and a freshly baked pie ready for anyone in need. However, as the years passed, Loreli outlived her husband and many of her children, a sorrow that weighed heavily on her heart.

In her twilight years, Loreli felt a calling to leave her village and share her healing gifts with the wider world. She joined an adventuring party, seeing in them the potential for good and the need for a guiding, nurturing presence. Despite her age, Loreli's spirit remained youthful and her faith unwavering.

Loreli has a peculiar habit of adopting everyone she meets as her “grandchildren.” This endearing quirk has led her to believe that one of her fellow adventurers is her actual granddaughter, despite there being no blood relation. She dotes on this adventurer, often fussing over them and offering sage advice, much to the amusement (and occasional exasperation) of the party.
