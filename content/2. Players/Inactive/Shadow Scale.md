### Backstory
Elarael was no ordinary elf. A ranger of the ancient forest of Eldrin, he'd spent his life in harmony with nature. Yet, a deep-seated restlessness gnawed at him. He felt a connection to something more profound, a power beyond the confines of his elven form.

One fateful night, while patrolling the forest's edge, Elarael stumbled upon a peculiar shrine. It was dedicated to [[Bahamut]], the Platinum Dragon, a deity known for his wisdom and courage. A surge of energy pulsed through the shrine, and Elarael felt an irresistible pull.

Driven by an unknown force, he stepped into the shrine and performed the Rite of Rebirth. The pain was excruciating, his body wracked with fire and lightning. But when the ordeal ended, Elarael emerged transformed. His elven form had been replaced by that of a dragonborn, his scales glistening like polished obsidian, reflecting a spectrum of colors in the light.

The transformation was more than physical. Elarael's mind was infused with the wisdom and courage of the Platinum Dragon. His connection to nature deepened, but he now saw it through a broader lens. He understood the interconnectedness of all living things, from the tiniest creature to the mightiest dragon.

As a dragonborn ranger, Elarael's skills were amplified. His senses became sharper, his reflexes quicker, and his endurance greater. He could commune with animals on a deeper level, understanding their thoughts and emotions. But most importantly, he had gained a new purpose. He was now a guardian of the natural world, a champion of Bahamut, sworn to protect the innocent and uphold the balance of nature.

With his newfound strength and wisdom, Elarael became a legendary figure in the land. His dark scales, a stark contrast to the platinum hue of [[Bahamut]], earned him the moniker "Shadow Scale." Known for his unwavering courage and his deep connection to the natural world, Shadow Scale became a symbol of hope and protection for all who encountered him..
