### Backstory
While on a recruitment quest in Neverwinter, a forest elf named Laucian came upon a toddler Tiefling wandering the streets. After keeping watch upon the her for several hours; Laucian realized the disdain others showed for her. A need to protect the child came over Laucian and after searching for several days for her parents, he realized she was alone. His decision to take her in concluded his quest and Laucian returned to the High Forest with the Tiefling in tow.  

Upon his return to Teuveamanthaar; Laucian introduced the teifling as his new recruit and adopted daughter, Kallíspallí. Initially, his introduction was met with question, and worry which  was quickly cast aside with confirmation from the elders. Kallí was raised knowing only love and protection from the members of the Tall Trees. 

Her place amongst the group began in the protection and preservation of small oaks. Growth and expansion of the forest is a vital part of protecting the ecosystem of the High Forest. In order to become a true member of the druid circle she must complete several tasks to prove her dedication to the cause.  

Upon her 25 year with the circle, Kallí ventured forth from the High Forest on her final task. An undertaking of momentous proportions. Cataloguing and studying 151 monsters within faerun in an attempt to create a comprehensive understanding of how different creatures benefit or harm the ecosystems of their dwelling.  

As she gathered her things, bag, notebook, utensils, rations, containment bottles and quarterstaff; the shine of her silver mirror-like eyes reflected the only home she'd ever truly known. Her father and family giving her only love, guidance, and purpose until this day. Upon this day a warning of the outside world "keep your head down, don't mind the stares, you know who.
