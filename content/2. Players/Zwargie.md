## Backstory

Zwarg does not remember much other than waking in the woods and hearing a voice in his head to join a group of adventurers. He doesn't remember being a part of the Order of Enlightened Orcs (Where he learned how to be a paladin), but luckily he can use his abilities innately. He has an item in his bag that is pulsing red with swirling black smoke inside. Zwarg does not know what this item is or how it got there. He just knows that he must keep it hidden and when he looks upon it, it brings great shame.
